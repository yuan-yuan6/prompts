## Best Practices

1. **Start with structure** - Don't write scenes before planning overall arc
2. **Write for the platform** - Consider where video will be viewed
3. **Show don't tell** - Use visual storytelling over exposition
4. **Test early and often** - Get feedback before finalizing
5. **Format properly** - Follow industry standards for professionalism
6. **Plan for production** - Consider feasibility while writing
7. **Optimize for engagement** - Focus on audience retention
8. **Iterate and improve** - Use data to refine approach
9. **Stay true to vision** - Balance feedback with creative intent
10. **Document everything** - Maintain clear records and versions
