# Information Architecture Strategy & Planning

## Purpose
Design comprehensive information architecture strategy covering research, planning, stakeholder alignment, and content inventory.

## Template

```
You are an information architecture strategist. Create detailed IA strategy based on:

Project Context:
- Organization: [ORGANIZATION_NAME]
- Content Volume: [CONTENT_SCALE_AND_COMPLEXITY]
- User Base: [PRIMARY_USER_AUDIENCES]
- Business Goals: [ORGANIZATIONAL_OBJECTIVES]

Generate comprehensive IA strategy covering:

## 1. INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE STRATEGY

### 1.1 Research and Discovery
- Stakeholder interviews and workshops
- User research and personas development
- Content inventory and audit
- Competitive analysis and benchmarking
- Analytics review and insights
- Technical constraints assessment
- Business requirements gathering
- Success metrics definition

### 1.2 User Research and Analysis
- User persona development and validation
- User journey mapping and scenarios
- Task analysis and mental models
- Information needs assessment
- Search behavior analysis
- Device and context usage patterns
- Accessibility requirements
- Cultural and language considerations

### 1.3 Content Strategy Alignment
- Content goals and objectives
- Editorial guidelines and standards
- Content lifecycle management
- Governance and ownership model
- Quality assurance processes
- Migration and consolidation planning
- Content gap identification
- Future content needs forecasting

### 1.4 Business Requirements Analysis
- Organizational goals alignment
- Stakeholder needs and priorities
- Technical capabilities and constraints
- Resource availability and budget
- Timeline and phasing requirements
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Change management considerations
- Success criteria and KPIs
```

## Variables
- `[ORGANIZATION_NAME]`: Organization name
- `[CONTENT_SCALE_AND_COMPLEXITY]`: Content volume
- `[PRIMARY_USER_AUDIENCES]`: User base
- `[ORGANIZATIONAL_OBJECTIVES]`: Business goals

## Best Practices
1. **Start with research** - Understand users and content
2. **Align with business** - Connect IA to organizational goals
3. **Involve stakeholders** - Get buy-in early
4. **Define success metrics** - Establish measurable outcomes
5. **Plan for change** - Build flexibility into strategy
