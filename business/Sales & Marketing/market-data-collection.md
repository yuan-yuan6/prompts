# Market Data Collection

## Purpose
Execute primary and secondary data collection using surveys, interviews, focus groups, observation, and desk research to gather comprehensive market intelligence.

## Template

```
### 1. SURVEY RESEARCH
Survey Design:
- Survey type: [SURVEY_TYPE] (Online/Phone/Mail/Face-to-face)
- Question count: [QUESTION_COUNT] questions
- Completion time: [COMPLETION_TIME] minutes
- Response scale: [RESPONSE_SCALE]

Question Types:
- Closed-ended: [CLOSED_ENDED_COUNT]
- Open-ended: [OPEN_ENDED_COUNT]
- Rating scales: [RATING_SCALE_COUNT]
- Matrix questions: [MATRIX_COUNT]

### 2. INTERVIEW RESEARCH
In-Depth Interviews:
- Interview type: [INTERVIEW_TYPE] (Structured/Semi-structured/Unstructured)
- Duration: [INTERVIEW_DURATION] minutes
- Sample size: [INTERVIEW_SAMPLE_SIZE] participants
- Recording method: [RECORDING_METHOD]

Interview Guide:
- Opening questions: [OPENING_QUESTIONS]
- Core questions: [CORE_INTERVIEW_QUESTIONS]
- Probing techniques: [PROBING_TECHNIQUES]
- Closing protocol: [CLOSING_PROTOCOL]

### 3. FOCUS GROUPS
Group Configuration:
- Group size: [FOCUS_GROUP_SIZE] participants
- Number of groups: [NUMBER_OF_GROUPS]
- Duration: [FOCUS_GROUP_DURATION] minutes
- Moderator: [MODERATOR_SELECTION]

Discussion Topics:
- Topic 1: [FOCUS_GROUP_TOPIC_1]
- Topic 2: [FOCUS_GROUP_TOPIC_2]
- Topic 3: [FOCUS_GROUP_TOPIC_3]

### 4. OBSERVATIONAL RESEARCH
Observation Type:
- Ethnographic studies: [ETHNOGRAPHIC_APPROACH]
- Mystery shopping: [MYSTERY_SHOPPING_PROTOCOL]
- Digital behavior: [DIGITAL_BEHAVIOR_TRACKING]

### 5. SECONDARY RESEARCH
Data Sources:
- Industry reports: [INDUSTRY_REPORT_SOURCES]
- Government data: [GOVERNMENT_DATA_SOURCES]
- Trade publications: [TRADE_PUBLICATION_SOURCES]
- Academic research: [ACADEMIC_SOURCES]
- Company filings: [COMPANY_FILING_SOURCES]
```

## Best Practices
- Pilot test all instruments
- Train data collectors thoroughly
- Monitor data quality continuously
- Ensure ethical research practices
- Protect participant privacy
- Maintain detailed documentation
- Use multiple data sources for triangulation
