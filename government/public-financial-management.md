# Public Financial Management & Budgeting

## Purpose
Framework for government budget development, execution, financial controls, fiscal transparency, performance-based budgeting, and public financial accountability.

## Quick Start

### Minimal Example
```
Develop annual budget for city government ($500M) including departmental budgets, capital projects, revenue forecasting, public hearings, council approval, execution controls, and performance measurement aligned with strategic priorities.
```

### Basic 3-Step Workflow
1. **Budget Development** - Forecasting, priorities, allocation (3-4 months)
2. **Approval Process** - Public input, council review, adoption (2-3 months)
3. **Execution & Monitoring** - Controls, reporting, adjustments (fiscal year)
