# Donor Cultivation & Stewardship

## Purpose
Comprehensive framework for cultivating donor relationships, major gift solicitation, donor retention strategies, stewardship programs, and building lifetime philanthropic partnerships.

## Quick Start

**Need to cultivate major donors?** Use this minimal example:

### Minimal Example
```
Develop donor cultivation plan for 50 major donor prospects ($10K+ capacity). Include research and qualification, personalized engagement strategies, moves management system, cultivation events, solicitation preparation, and stewardship program to ensure 75%+ retention.
```

### When to Use This
- Building major donor pipeline
- Improving donor retention rates
- Developing stewardship programs
- Planning cultivation events
- Strengthening donor relationships

### Basic 3-Step Workflow
1. **Identify & Research** - Prospect research, capacity rating (2-4 weeks)
2. **Cultivate & Engage** - Personalized touches, relationship building (3-12 months)
3. **Steward & Retain** - Impact reporting, recognition, ongoing engagement

**Time to complete**: Ongoing relationship management, major gift cycle 12-24 months

## Best Practices

- **Donor-Centered** - Focus on donor motivations, not just needs
- **Authentic Relationships** - Genuine interest and connection
- **Listen First** - Understand before asking
- **Prompt Thanks** - Acknowledge immediately and personally
- **Report Impact** - Show how gifts make a difference
- **Multiple Touches** - 7+ meaningful interactions annually
- **Consistency Matters** - Follow through and reliability
- **Think Long-term** - Build lifetime relationships
