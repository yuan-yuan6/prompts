## Variables
- `[EXISTING_HEALTH_IT_SYSTEMS]`: Current systems
- `[ARTIFICIAL_INTELLIGENCE_READINESS]`: AI maturity
- `[SYSTEM_CONNECTIVITY_REQUIREMENTS]`: Integration needs
- `[DATA_QUALITY_GOVERNANCE]`: Data infrastructure

## Best Practices
1. **Start with use cases** - Identify high-value AI applications
2. **Ensure data quality** - Clean and validate training data
3. **Validate rigorously** - Test AI models clinically
4. **Monitor continuously** - Track AI performance
5. **Integrate seamlessly** - Embed in clinical workflows
