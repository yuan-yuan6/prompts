# Market Research Design

## Purpose
Design rigorous research studies with appropriate methodology, sampling strategy, and measurement frameworks to answer key business questions.

## Template

```
Research Context:
- Industry: [INDUSTRY_SECTOR]
- Market position: [CURRENT_MARKET_POSITION]
- Research budget: $[RESEARCH_BUDGET_ALLOCATION]
- Timeline: [RESEARCH_TIMELINE]

### 1. RESEARCH OBJECTIVES
Primary Objectives: [PRIMARY_RESEARCH_GOALS]
Secondary Objectives: [SECONDARY_RESEARCH_GOALS]
Business Questions: [KEY_BUSINESS_QUESTIONS]
Success Metrics: [RESEARCH_SUCCESS_CRITERIA]

### 2. RESEARCH METHODOLOGY
Research Philosophy:
- Approach: [RESEARCH_APPROACH] (Exploratory/Descriptive/Causal)
- Design: [RESEARCH_DESIGN] (Cross-sectional/Longitudinal)
- Methods: [RESEARCH_METHODS] (Quantitative/Qualitative/Mixed)

### 3. SAMPLING STRATEGY
Population Definition:
- Target population: [TARGET_POPULATION]
- Sampling frame: [SAMPLING_FRAME]
- Sample size: [SAMPLE_SIZE] participants
- Sampling method: [SAMPLING_METHOD]

### 4. DATA COLLECTION PLAN
Primary Research:
- Surveys: [SURVEY_METHODOLOGY]
- Interviews: [INTERVIEW_APPROACH]
- Focus groups: [FOCUS_GROUP_PLAN]
- Observations: [OBSERVATION_METHOD]

Secondary Research:
- Industry reports: [INDUSTRY_REPORT_SOURCES]
- Market data: [MARKET_DATA_SOURCES]
- Competitive intelligence: [COMPETITIVE_INTEL_SOURCES]
```

## Best Practices
- Align methodology with research questions
- Ensure adequate sample size for statistical power
- Use mixed methods for comprehensive insights
- Pilot test instruments before full deployment
- Account for potential biases in design
- Document all methodological decisions
