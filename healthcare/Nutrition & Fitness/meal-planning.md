# Meal Planning Template

## Overview
This comprehensive template assists nutrition professionals, healthcare providers, and individuals in creating personalized, evidence-based meal plans that optimize nutritional outcomes, support health goals, and accommodate individual preferences and constraints.

## Template Structure

### 1. Individual Assessment and Nutritional Profiling

**Comprehensive Nutrition Assessment Framework:**
```
As a [NUTRITION_PROFESSIONAL_ROLE] with [CERTIFICATION_TYPE] working in [PRACTICE_SETTING] with [YEARS_EXPERIENCE] years of experience, I am developing a meal plan for [CLIENT_AGE]-year-old [CLIENT_GENDER] [CLIENT_ETHNICITY] with [PRIMARY_HEALTH_GOAL].

Anthropometric Data: Height [HEIGHT_MEASUREMENT], Weight [CURRENT_WEIGHT], BMI [BMI_CALCULATION], Waist circumference [WAIST_MEASUREMENT], Body fat percentage [BODY_FAT_PERCENTAGE], Muscle mass [MUSCLE_MASS_MEASUREMENT].

Metabolic Parameters: Resting Metabolic Rate [RMR_CALCULATION], Total Daily Energy Expenditure [TDEE_CALCULATION], Activity Factor [ACTIVITY_MULTIPLIER], Weight change goal [WEIGHT_GOAL_TIMELINE].

Medical History: [PRIMARY_MEDICAL_CONDITION], [SECONDARY_CONDITIONS_LIST], [MEDICATION_LIST], [SUPPLEMENT_REGIMEN], [ALLERGIES_INTOLERANCES], [DIGESTIVE_ISSUES], [CHRONIC_DISEASES], [METABOLIC_DISORDERS].

Dietary Assessment: Current eating pattern [EATING_PATTERN_TYPE], Meal frequency [MEALS_PER_DAY], Typical portions [PORTION_SIZE_ASSESSMENT], Food preferences [PREFERRED_FOODS_LIST], Dislikes [AVOIDED_FOODS_LIST], Cooking skills [CULINARY_SKILL_LEVEL].

Lifestyle Factors: Work schedule [WORK_SCHEDULE_TYPE], Exercise routine [EXERCISE_FREQUENCY], Sleep pattern [SLEEP_HOURS_QUALITY], Stress level [STRESS_ASSESSMENT_SCALE], Social eating habits [SOCIAL_EATING_FREQUENCY], Budget constraints [FOOD_BUDGET_WEEKLY].

Cultural and Religious Considerations: [CULTURAL_FOOD_TRADITIONS], [RELIGIOUS_DIETARY_LAWS], [HOLIDAY_MEAL_CUSTOMS], [FAMILY_FOOD_PRACTICES], [COMMUNITY_EATING_PATTERNS].

Behavioral Patterns: Emotional eating triggers [EMOTIONAL_EATING_TRIGGERS], Meal timing patterns [MEAL_TIMING_PREFERENCES], Snacking habits [SNACKING_FREQUENCY], Hydration patterns [DAILY_FLUID_INTAKE], Mindful eating practices [MINDFULNESS_LEVEL].
```
