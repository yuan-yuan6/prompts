# Recruitment - Overview & Navigation

## Purpose
Navigate the recruitment prompt library and understand how each focused prompt supports different stages of the hiring process.

## Quick Recruitment Navigation Prompt
Navigate recruitment process for [role type] at [company]. Stage: [define/source/screen/assess/optimize]. Use: job-analysis for descriptions → candidate-sourcing for pipelines → screening-interview for process → candidate-assessment for selection → recruitment-metrics for optimization. Priority: [urgent hire/pipeline building/process improvement]. Output: [deliverable needed].

## Recruitment Process Overview

The recruitment process consists of five interconnected stages, each with its own specialized prompt:

```
1. DEFINE → 2. SOURCE → 3. SCREEN → 4. ASSESS → 5. OPTIMIZE
   |           |           |           |            |
   Job        Sourcing    Interview   Selection    Metrics
  Analysis    & Brand     Process     & Assess     & Improve
```

## Prompt Library Navigation

### 1. Job Analysis & Role Definition
**File:** `job-analysis-definition.md`

**Use this when you need to:**
- Create comprehensive job descriptions
- Define role requirements and qualifications
- Establish competency frameworks
- Design success metrics for positions
- Clarify reporting structures and responsibilities

**Key outputs:**
- Complete job descriptions
- Required vs. preferred qualifications
- Technical and behavioral competency frameworks
- Success metrics and KPIs
- Career progression paths

**Best for:** HR business partners, hiring managers, talent acquisition specialists starting a new role definition

## How to Use This Library

### For a Complete Hiring Process
Use prompts **sequentially** from 1 → 5:

1. **Define the role** (job-analysis-definition.md)
2. **Source candidates** (candidate-sourcing-branding.md)
3. **Screen and interview** (screening-interview-process.md)
4. **Assess and select** (candidate-assessment-selection.md)
5. **Measure and optimize** (recruitment-metrics-optimization.md)

### For Specific Challenges
Select the **individual prompt** that addresses your immediate need:

| **Challenge** | **Use This Prompt** |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Unclear job requirements | Job Analysis & Role Definition |
| Can't find qualified candidates | Candidate Sourcing & Branding |
| Inconsistent interview process | Screening & Interview Process |
| Difficulty choosing between candidates | Candidate Assessment & Selection |
| Don't know if recruiting is effective | Recruitment Metrics & Optimization |
| Poor candidate experience | Screening & Interview + Assessment & Selection |
| Low offer acceptance rates | Candidate Sourcing & Branding |
| High new hire turnover | Job Analysis + Assessment & Selection + Metrics |
| Diversity hiring goals not met | All prompts (each has D&I focus) |

### For Different Roles

**Hiring Managers:**
- Start with: Job Analysis & Role Definition
- Then use: Screening & Interview Process
- Finally: Candidate Assessment & Selection

**Recruiters/Talent Acquisition:**
- Start with: Candidate Sourcing & Branding
- Core tools: Screening & Interview + Assessment & Selection
- Track with: Recruitment Metrics & Optimization

**HR Leaders/Executives:**
- Strategic view: Recruitment Metrics & Optimization
- Process design: All prompts for system building
- Team training: Screening & Interview + Assessment & Selection

**Small Business Owners (wearing all hats):**
- Use all prompts sequentially for first hires
- Focus on: Job Analysis + Screening & Interview for efficiency
