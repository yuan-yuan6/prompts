## How to Use This Series

### For Comprehensive Implementation
1. **Start with Part 1** to establish governance, risk appetite, and credit risk foundation
2. **Continue to Part 2** for market and operational risk frameworks
3. **Complete with Part 3** for regulatory compliance and innovation capabilities

### For Targeted Implementation
Choose the part(s) most relevant to your immediate needs:

- **Governance Focus:** Part 1
- **Trading/Market Risk:** Part 2
- **Compliance/Regulatory:** Part 3
- **Innovation/Analytics:** Part 3
- **Operational Resilience:** Part 2
- **Credit Portfolio:** Part 1

### For Specific Institution Types

**Community Banks ($500M - $10B assets):**
- Essential: Parts 1 & 3 (foundation, credit, compliance)
- Optional: Part 2 (if significant trading/ALM operations)

**Regional Banks ($10B - $50B assets):**
- All three parts recommended
- Emphasis on Parts 1 & 2 for core operations
- Part 3 for regulatory sophistication

**Large Banks ($50B+ assets):**
- All three parts required
- Full implementation of regulatory requirements
- Advanced analytics and innovation from Part 3

**Investment Firms:**
- Essential: Parts 2 & 3 (market risk, compliance)
- Foundation: Part 1 (governance, culture)

**Credit Unions:**
- Essential: Parts 1 & 3 (foundation, compliance)
- Selective: Part 2 (operational risk sections)

## Implementation Approach

### Phased Implementation (Recommended)

**Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-12)**
- Use Part 1 to establish governance and risk appetite
- Initial credit risk framework
- Reference Part 3 for regulatory requirements

**Phase 2: Core Risk Programs (Weeks 13-24)**
- Implement remaining Part 1 credit components
- Deploy Part 2 market and operational frameworks
- Part 3 model governance foundation

**Phase 3: Integration (Weeks 25-36)**
- Complete Part 2 operational risk controls
- Part 3 regulatory compliance systems
- Enterprise risk integration across all parts

**Phase 4: Enhancement (Weeks 37-48)**
- Part 3 advanced analytics and innovation
- Climate risk and ESG from Part 3
- Continuous improvement across all areas

### Parallel Implementation (For Larger Institutions)

Run multiple workstreams simultaneously:
- **Workstream 1:** Governance & Culture (Part 1)
- **Workstream 2:** Credit Risk (Part 1)
- **Workstream 3:** Market Risk (Part 2)
- **Workstream 4:** Operational Risk (Part 2)
- **Workstream 5:** Compliance & Regulatory (Part 3)
- **Workstream 6:** Advanced Analytics (Part 3)

## Common Use Cases

### Use Case 1: New Risk Framework Development
**Recommended Path:** Parts 1 → 2 → 3 (sequential)
- Start with governance and foundation
- Build out risk measurement capabilities
- Add compliance and innovation

### Use Case 2: Regulatory Examination Preparation
**Recommended Path:** Parts 1 & 3 (governance + compliance)
- Strengthen governance (Part 1)
- Ensure regulatory compliance (Part 3)
- Review operational controls (Part 2)

### Use Case 3: Digital Transformation
**Recommended Path:** Part 3 → Part 2 (innovation + operations)
- Digital risk framework (Part 3)
- Technology and cybersecurity (Part 2)
- Risk culture adaptation (Part 1)

**Recommended Path:** All parts with focus on integration

- Compliance integration (Part 3)

**Recommended Path:** Part 1 (credit) + Part 3 (models)
- Credit risk framework (Part 1)
- Model development and validation (Part 3)
- Risk appetite adjustment (Part 1)
