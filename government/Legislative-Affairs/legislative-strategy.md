# Legislative Affairs & Bill Analysis

## Purpose
Comprehensive framework for legislative advocacy, bill analysis, stakeholder coalition building, lobbying strategy, testimony preparation, and navigating the legislative process.

## Quick Start

**Need to influence legislation?** Use this minimal example:

### Minimal Example
```
Develop legislative strategy to support healthcare reform bill in state legislature. Analyze bill provisions, identify supporters/opponents, build coalition of stakeholders, prepare testimony, mobilize grassroots advocacy, and track bill progress through committee and floor votes.
```

### When to Use This
- Advocating for or against legislation
- Analyzing policy proposals
- Building legislative coalitions
- Preparing legislative testimony
- Government affairs and lobbying

### Basic 3-Step Workflow
1. **Analysis & Strategy** - Bill analysis, stakeholder mapping (1-2 weeks)
2. **Advocacy Campaign** - Coalition building, lobbying, grassroots (ongoing)
3. **Monitoring & Adaptation** - Track progress, adjust tactics (throughout session)

**Time to complete**: Legislative session (months), ongoing advocacy

## Best Practices

- **Know the Process** - Understand legislative rules and procedures
- **Build Relationships** - Invest in long-term legislator relationships
- **Data-Driven** - Use evidence and research
- **Tell Stories** - Personal narratives move hearts and minds
- **Be Credible** - Provide accurate, balanced information
- **Respect Process** - Work within system, don't circumvent
- **Persistence** - Legislative change takes time
- **Adaptability** - Adjust tactics as circumstances change
