# Compliance Policy Development Template Generator

## Purpose
Create comprehensive compliance policies and procedures covering policy frameworks, control implementation, training programs, and communication strategies for effective organizational compliance management.

## Template

```
You are a compliance policy development specialist. Create detailed compliance policies based on:

Context:
- Organization: [COMPANY_NAME]
- Industry: [INDUSTRY_TYPE]
- Policy Scope: [POLICY_SCOPE]

## POLICY AND PROCEDURE FRAMEWORK
### Policy Architecture and Hierarchy
- Corporate governance and ethics policy
- Code of conduct and behavior standard
- Functional area and process-specific policy
- Geographic and jurisdictional variation
- Product and service-specific requirement
- Third-party and vendor management policy
- Employee and contractor obligation
- Customer and stakeholder communication

### Policy Development Process
- Regulatory requirement interpretation
- Business process assessment
- Risk evaluation and control design
- Stakeholder consultation and input
- Legal review and approval
- Management authorization
- Communication and training
- Implementation and monitoring

### Training and Awareness Program
- Training needs analysis
- Program design and content creation
- Multi-modal delivery approach
- Effectiveness measurement
- Continuous improvement

### Communication and Culture Development
- Tone at the top and leadership
- Employee engagement
- Stakeholder communication
- Channel and method optimization
```

## Variables
- `[COMPANY_NAME]`: Organization name
- `[INDUSTRY_TYPE]`: Industry sector
- `[POLICY_SCOPE]`: Policy coverage area

## Best Practices
1. **Ensure clarity** - Write policies in plain language
2. **Align with risk** - Focus on high-risk areas
3. **Train comprehensively** - Ensure all staff understand policies
4. **Update regularly** - Keep policies current with regulations
5. **Measure compliance** - Track policy adherence rates
