# Patient Care Plans - Comprehensive Prompt Template

## Introduction
This comprehensive prompt template enables healthcare professionals to create detailed, personalized patient care plans incorporating clinical assessments, treatment protocols, interdisciplinary coordination, and outcome tracking across various healthcare settings and specialties.

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Complex Medical Patient
**Context**: 68-year-old with multiple chronic conditions requiring coordinated care

**Implementation**:
- Assessment: Comprehensive evaluation of diabetes, heart failure, COPD, and renal insufficiency
- Goals: Stabilize blood glucose <180mg/dL, reduce readmissions, improve functional capacity
- Interventions: Medication optimization, dietary consultation, cardiac rehabilitation, pulmonary therapy
- Monitoring: Daily weights, blood glucose QID, oxygen saturation monitoring, weekly labs
- Education: Disease management, medication adherence, symptom recognition, dietary restrictions

### Example 2: Post-Surgical Recovery
**Context**: 45-year-old following major abdominal surgery with complications

**Implementation**:
- Assessment: Post-operative status, wound healing, pain levels, infection risk
- Goals: Wound healing within 4 weeks, pain control <4/10, return to baseline function
- Interventions: Wound care protocols, pain management regimen, physical therapy, nutritional support
- Monitoring: Wound assessment BID, vital signs Q4H, drain output, laboratory markers
- Discharge Planning: Home health referral, outpatient therapy, follow-up appointments scheduled

### Example 3: Pediatric Chronic Care
**Context**: 8-year-old with newly diagnosed Type 1 diabetes requiring family education

**Implementation**:
- Assessment: Glucose control, growth parameters, psychosocial adjustment, family dynamics
- Goals: Maintain HbA1c <7.5%, prevent DKA, promote normal development, school integration
- Interventions: Insulin therapy, carbohydrate counting, continuous glucose monitoring, school planning
- Education: Family diabetes education, school nurse training, emergency management, peer support
- Follow-up: Endocrinology every 3 months, nutrition quarterly, psychology as needed

## Customization Options

### Option 1: Acute Care Focus
Emphasize rapid stabilization, intensive monitoring, critical interventions, emergency protocols, and ICU-level care planning with hourly assessments and immediate response capabilities.

### Option 2: Chronic Disease Management
Focus on long-term disease control, lifestyle modifications, self-management support, prevention of complications, and quality of life optimization with quarterly evaluations.

### Option 3: Rehabilitation Planning
Prioritize functional recovery, therapy coordination, adaptive equipment, independence goals, and community reintegration with weekly progress assessments and milestone tracking.

### Option 4: Palliative Care Approach
Center on comfort measures, symptom management, quality of life, family support, advance care planning, and end-of-life preparations with daily comfort assessments.

### Option 5: Preventive Care Strategy
Emphasize health promotion, risk reduction, screening protocols, immunizations, lifestyle counseling, and early intervention with annual comprehensive assessments and targeted interventions.
