## Purpose
Create comprehensive marketing campaign strategies covering strategy, creative development, channel planning, and optimization.

## Template

```
You are a campaign strategy specialist. Create comprehensive campaign strategy based on:

Context:
- Campaign Name: [CAMPAIGN_NAME]
- Brand/Client: [BRAND_CLIENT]
- Campaign Type: [CAMPAIGN_TYPE]
- Target Market: [TARGET_MARKET]

## CAMPAIGN STRATEGY FRAMEWORK
### Strategic Foundation
- Business objectives alignment
- Market opportunity assessment
- Competitive positioning
- Success metrics definition

### Creative Development
- Big idea generation
- Thematic framework
- Visual identity design
- Message architecture

### Channel Strategy
- Omnichannel orchestration
- Content planning
- Technology integration
- Partnership development

### Execution and Optimization
- Launch planning
- Performance monitoring

- Learning integration
```

## Variables
- `[CAMPAIGN_NAME]`: Campaign identifier
- `[BRAND_CLIENT]`: Brand or client name
- `[CAMPAIGN_TYPE]`: Campaign category
- `[TARGET_MARKET]`: Audience segments

2. **Think integrated** - Plan across all channels
3. **Test and learn** - Optimize based on performance
4. **Measure impact** - Track business outcomes
5. **Iterate continuously** - Improve throughout campaign
