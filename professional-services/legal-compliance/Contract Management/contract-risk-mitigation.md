## Variables
- `[COMPANY_NAME]`: Organization name and identity
- `[INDUSTRY_TYPE]`: Industry sector and business classification
- `[RISK_APPETITE]`: Risk tolerance and management approach
- `[OPERATIONAL_GEOGRAPHY]`: Geographic scope and locations
- `[RISK_MANAGEMENT_MATURITY]`: Current risk management capability
- `[CONTRACT_CATEGORY]`: Type and classification of contract
- `[FINANCIAL_VALUE]`: Contract value and financial impact
- `[RISK_LEVEL]`: Risk profile and exposure level
- `[CONTRACT_COMPLEXITY]`: Complexity level and sophistication

## Best Practices

1. **Identify early and often** - Continuous risk identification throughout contract lifecycle
2. **Quantify where possible** - Use data and analytics to assess risk magnitude
3. **Prioritize ruthlessly** - Focus resources on highest-impact risks
4. **Build in flexibility** - Design adaptive strategies that can evolve
5. **Monitor continuously** - Don't set and forget risk mitigation plans
6. **Communicate transparently** - Share risk information with stakeholders
7. **Learn from experience** - Capture and apply lessons learned
8. **Balance cost and protection** - Right-size mitigation to risk level

## Tips for Success

- Use multiple risk assessment methods for comprehensive coverage
- Involve subject matter experts in risk identification and assessment
- Create clear ownership and accountability for risk mitigation
- Build risk discussions into regular governance meetings
- Use technology for real-time risk monitoring and alerts
- Test contingency plans through simulations and exercises
- Maintain updated risk registers with current information
- Foster a risk-aware culture throughout the organization
