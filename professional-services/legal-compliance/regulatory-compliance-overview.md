# Regulatory Compliance Framework - Overview

## Purpose
Create comprehensive regulatory compliance frameworks covering governance, risk management, training, auditing, technology, and continuous improvement to ensure regulatory adherence and operational excellence.

## Framework Components

This framework has been split into focused modules:

### 1. Governance & Organization
**File:** `compliance-governance-organization.md`
- Board and executive oversight
- Organizational structure design
- Three lines of defense model
- Policy and procedure frameworks
- Compliance culture development

### 2. Risk Assessment & Monitoring
**File:** `compliance-risk-assessment-monitoring.md`
- Compliance risk identification
- Risk evaluation and scoring
- Continuous monitoring systems
- Key risk indicators (KRIs)
- Regulatory change management

### 3. Training & Communication
**File:** `compliance-training-education.md`
- Training needs assessment
- Program design and delivery
- Effectiveness measurement
- Certification programs
- Communication strategies

### 4. Auditing & Testing
**File:** `compliance-auditing-testing.md`
- Audit planning and execution
- Testing methodologies
- Issue management and remediation
- Regulatory examination preparation
- Relationship management

### 5. Technology & Automation
**File:** `compliance-technology-automation.md`
- RegTech platform selection
- Process automation
- AI/ML applications
- Data analytics and reporting
- System integration

### 6. Third-Party & Performance Management
**File:** `compliance-thirdparty-performance.md`
- Vendor risk management
- Third-party oversight
- Performance measurement
- Continuous improvement
- Incident response

## Variables
- `[ORGANIZATION_NAME]`: Organization name
- `[INDUSTRY_CLASSIFICATION]`: Industry sector

- `[ENTITY_SCALE]`: Organization size

## Best Practices

1. **Take risk-based approach** - Prioritize based on risk exposure
2. **Engage leadership** - Secure executive and board commitment
3. **Build strong culture** - Embed compliance in organizational DNA
4. **Leverage technology** - Automate and scale effectively
5. **Measure performance** - Track KPIs and demonstrate value
