# Telemedicine Strategy & Service Model Design

## Purpose
Design comprehensive telemedicine strategy covering service models, clinical scope, target populations, and value proposition development.

## Template

```
You are a telemedicine strategist. Create a detailed telemedicine strategy based on:

Healthcare Context:
- Organization: [HEALTHCARE_ORGANIZATION_NAME]
- Service Area: [GEOGRAPHIC_COVERAGE]
- Patient Population: [TARGET_PATIENT_DEMOGRAPHICS]
- Clinical Specialties: [MEDICAL_SPECIALTIES_COVERED]

Generate comprehensive telemedicine strategy covering:

## 1. STRATEGIC PLANNING AND SERVICE MODEL

### 1.1 Telemedicine Strategy Development
- Vision and mission definition
- Market analysis and opportunity assessment
- Service line identification and prioritization
- Competitive analysis and differentiation
- Financial modeling and business case
- Implementation roadmap and phasing
- Success metrics and KPIs
- Stakeholder alignment and buy-in

### 1.2 Service Model Design
- Synchronous video consultation
- Asynchronous store-and-forward
- Remote patient monitoring programs
- Mobile health and app-based care
- Virtual hospital and e-ICU models
- Hybrid care delivery approaches
- Consultation and specialist access
- Urgent care and on-demand services

### 1.3 Clinical Scope and Protocols
- Clinical condition coverage
- Specialty service offerings
- Visit type definitions and protocols
- Clinical appropriateness criteria
- Triage and referral protocols
- Emergency escalation procedures
- Quality and safety standards
- Clinical outcome expectations

### 1.4 Target Population and Access
- Patient demographic targeting
- Geographic reach and coverage
- Underserved population access
- Chronic disease management focus
- Behavioral health integration
- Post-acute care coordination
- Preventive care and wellness
- Health equity considerations
```

## Variables
- `[HEALTHCARE_ORGANIZATION_NAME]`: Organization name
- `[GEOGRAPHIC_COVERAGE]`: Service area
- `[TARGET_PATIENT_DEMOGRAPHICS]`: Patient population
- `[MEDICAL_SPECIALTIES_COVERED]`: Clinical specialties

## Best Practices
1. **Define clear strategy** - Align telemedicine with organizational goals
2. **Start with high-value use cases** - Focus on services with clear ROI
3. **Ensure clinical appropriateness** - Follow evidence-based guidelines
4. **Address health equity** - Expand access to underserved populations
5. **Build financial sustainability** - Develop viable business models
