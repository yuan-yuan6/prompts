# Risk Mitigation & Management

## Purpose
Develop risk mitigation strategies and management frameworks including risk controls, hedging strategies, insurance, and continuous monitoring.

## Template

```
You are a risk mitigation specialist. Create risk mitigation framework for:

Context:
- Identified Risks: [RISK_LIST]
- Risk Tolerance: [RISK_TOLERANCE]
- Budget: [MITIGATION_BUDGET]

Generate comprehensive risk mitigation plan:

## MITIGATION STRATEGIES

### Risk Avoidance
- Eliminate risk exposure
- Exit risky activities
- Policy restrictions
- Activity prohibition

### Risk Reduction
- Process improvements
- Control enhancements
- System upgrades
- Training programs
- Quality assurance
- Compliance programs

### Risk Transfer
- Insurance coverage
- Hedging strategies
- Contractual transfer
- Outsourcing
- Derivatives

### Risk Acceptance
- Risk tolerance assessment
- Capital allocation
- Reserves and provisions
- Contingency planning

## CONTROL FRAMEWORK

### Preventive Controls
- Authorization procedures
- Segregation of duties
- Access controls
- Pre-approval processes

### Detective Controls
- Monitoring systems
- Reconciliations
- Audits and reviews
- Exception reporting

### Corrective Controls
- Incident response
- Recovery procedures
- Root cause analysis
- Process improvements

## MONITORING & REPORTING

### Continuous Monitoring
- KRI tracking
- Dashboard monitoring
- Alert systems
- Trend analysis

### Reporting Framework
- Risk reports
- Executive dashboards
- Board reporting
- Regulatory reporting

## DELIVERABLES

- Mitigation strategy document
- Control framework
- Monitoring system
- Reporting templates
- Incident response plans

Ensure mitigation is effective, cost-efficient, and sustainable.
```

## Usage Example
Use for risk mitigation planning, control design, or monitoring systems.
