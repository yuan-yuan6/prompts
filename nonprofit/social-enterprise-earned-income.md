# Social Enterprise & Earned Income

## Purpose
Framework for developing nonprofit earned income strategies, launching social enterprises, creating mission-aligned business ventures, and diversifying revenue beyond grants and donations.

## Quick Start

### Minimal Example
```
Develop social enterprise strategy for workforce development nonprofit including training cafe employing program participants, fee-for-service corporate training, product sales, facility rental, and consulting services generating 30% earned income revenue.
```

### Basic 3-Step Workflow
1. **Assess Opportunities** - Capabilities, market, mission alignment (4-8 weeks)
2. **Develop Business Plan** - Model, pricing, operations (2-3 months)
3. **Launch & Scale** - Pilot, refine, expand (12-24 months)

---

## Key Components

- Earned income opportunity assessment
- Social enterprise business planning
- Mission alignment evaluation
- Market and competitive analysis
- Financial modeling and pricing
- Legal structure (UBIT considerations)
- Operations and staffing
- Impact measurement
