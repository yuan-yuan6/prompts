## üí° Key Concepts

**Scope 1, 2, 3 Emissions:**
- **Scope 1:** Direct emissions (owned/controlled sources)
- **Scope 2:** Indirect emissions (purchased electricity, heat, cooling)
- **Scope 3:** Value chain emissions (suppliers, logistics, product use)

**ESG Pillars:**
- **Environmental:** Climate, waste, water, biodiversity
- **Social:** Labor, human rights, community, diversity
- **Governance:** Board structure, ethics, compliance, transparency

**Circular Economy Principles:**
- Design out waste and pollution
- Keep products and materials in use
- Regenerate natural systems

## üéØ Business Drivers

**Why ESG Matters:**

1. **Investor Expectations** - ESG affects valuations and access to capital
2. **Regulatory Requirements** - Increasing mandatory disclosure (CSRD, SEC)
3. **Customer Demand** - Consumers choose sustainable brands
4. **Talent Attraction** - Employees want responsible employers
5. **Risk Management** - Climate and social risks are business risks
6. **Competitive Advantage** - Sustainability as differentiation
7. **Cost Reduction** - Energy efficiency and waste reduction save money
