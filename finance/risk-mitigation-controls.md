## Purpose
Design comprehensive operational risk frameworks covering process controls, fraud prevention, technology risk, cybersecurity, and business continuity.

## 4. OPERATIONAL RISK MANAGEMENT

### 4.1 Operational Risk Framework

- Risk and control self-assessment (RCSA)
- Key risk indicator development
- Loss event and near-miss reporting
- Scenario analysis and stress testing
- Risk assessment and identification
- Loss data collection and analysis

- Operational risk capital calculation

- Control identification and effectiveness

- Management testing and certification

- Customer identification and verification
- Suspicious activity monitoring
- Currency transaction reporting
- Sanctions screening

### 4.4 Technology and Cybersecurity Risk
- IT risk assessment and governance

- Vendor and third-party risk management

- Threat intelligence and vulnerability management

## Variables
- `[COMMERCIAL_RETAIL_INVESTMENT_SERVICES]`: Business lines
- `[RISK_MANAGEMENT_TECHNOLOGY_PLATFORM]`: Technology infrastructure
- `[OPERATIONAL_SOPHISTICATION_LEVEL]`: Process maturity
- `[COMPLIANCE_REQUIREMENTS]`: Regulatory environment

## Best Practices
1. **Design strong controls** - Implement preventive and detective controls
2. **Test regularly** - Validate control effectiveness
3. **Monitor continuously** - Track KRIs and incidents
4. **Respond quickly** - Address issues promptly
5. **Learn from events** - Analyze and improve from incidents
