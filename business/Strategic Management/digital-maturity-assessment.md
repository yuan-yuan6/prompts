# Digital Maturity Assessment & Gap Analysis

## Purpose
Design comprehensive digital maturity assessment frameworks covering capability evaluation, gap analysis, benchmarking, and prioritization.

## Template

```
You are a digital maturity assessment specialist. Create detailed assessment framework based on:

Assessment Context:
- Current State: [DIGITAL_CAPABILITIES]
- Target State: [DESIRED_MATURITY_LEVEL]
- Focus Areas: [PRIORITY_DOMAINS]
- Timeline: [TRANSFORMATION_TIMEFRAME]

Generate comprehensive assessment guidance covering:

## 2. MATURITY ASSESSMENT AND GAP ANALYSIS

### 2.1 Digital Maturity Framework
- Strategy and leadership dimension
- Customer experience dimension
- Operations and processes dimension
- Technology and data dimension
- Organization and culture dimension
- Innovation and agility dimension
- Ecosystem and partnerships dimension
- Maturity level definitions and criteria

### 2.2 Current State Assessment
- Digital strategy clarity and execution
- Customer digital engagement and experience
- Process automation and digitization
- Technology architecture and capabilities
- Data management and analytics maturity
- Digital skills and talent capabilities
- Innovation processes and culture
- Change readiness and capacity

### 2.3 Gap Analysis and Prioritization
- Current vs. target state comparison
- Capability gaps identification
- Impact and effort assessment
- Quick wins and strategic bets
- Dependency mapping and sequencing
- Resource and investment requirements
- Risk evaluation and mitigation
- Roadmap development and phasing

### 2.4 Benchmarking and Best Practices
- Industry peer comparison
- Digital leaders analysis
- Best practice identification
- Technology trend evaluation
- Regulatory landscape assessment
- Competitive intelligence gathering
- Customer expectation analysis
- Emerging opportunity identification
```

## Variables
- `[DIGITAL_CAPABILITIES]`: Current state
- `[DESIRED_MATURITY_LEVEL]`: Target state
- `[PRIORITY_DOMAINS]`: Focus areas
- `[TRANSFORMATION_TIMEFRAME]`: Timeline

## Best Practices
1. **Assess honestly** - Conduct objective evaluation
2. **Benchmark externally** - Compare to leaders
3. **Prioritize ruthlessly** - Focus on highest impact
4. **Sequence logically** - Build on foundations
5. **Involve stakeholders** - Get diverse perspectives
