# Regulatory Reporting Template Generator

## Purpose
Create comprehensive regulatory reporting strategies covering incident management, response protocols, reporting obligations, and stakeholder communication.

## Template

```
You are a regulatory reporting specialist. Create comprehensive reporting strategy based on:

Context:
- Organization: [COMPANY_NAME]
- Regulatory Bodies: [REGULATORY_AUTHORITIES]
- Reporting Requirements: [REPORTING_OBLIGATIONS]

## INCIDENT MANAGEMENT AND RESPONSE
### Incident Detection and Reporting
- Multi-channel detection mechanisms
- Incident classification and triage
- Internal reporting and escalation
- External reporting and disclosure

### Investigation and Root Cause Analysis
- Investigation process and methodology
- Evidence collection and preservation
- Root cause analysis techniques
- Documentation and reporting

### Remediation and Corrective Action
- Immediate response and containment
- Long-term solution development
- Action plan execution
- Effectiveness validation

## REGULATORY REPORTING
### Management Reporting
- Executive dashboards
- Risk and compliance status
- Performance metrics
- Exception identification

### External Reporting
- Mandatory regulatory filings
- Voluntary disclosure initiatives
- Stakeholder communication
- Public reporting requirements
```

## Variables
- `[COMPANY_NAME]`: Organization name
- `[REGULATORY_AUTHORITIES]`: Relevant regulators
- `[REPORTING_OBLIGATIONS]`: Required reports

## Best Practices
1. **Report timely** - Meet all regulatory deadlines
2. **Ensure accuracy** - Verify all reported information
3. **Maintain transparency** - Provide complete disclosures
4. **Document thoroughly** - Keep comprehensive records
5. **Coordinate communication** - Align internal and external messaging
