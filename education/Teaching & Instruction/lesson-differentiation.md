# Lesson Differentiation

## Purpose
Adapt instruction to meet diverse learning needs, provide appropriate support and challenges, and ensure all students can access and master content.

## Best Practices
- Know your students well
- Differentiate proactively, not reactively
- Use flexible grouping
- Provide choice when possible
- Scaffold strategically
- Set high expectations for all
- Monitor progress continuously
- Collaborate with support staff
