# Competency Development Planning

## Purpose
Create comprehensive competency development plans including gap analysis, learning strategies, career pathways, succession planning, and progress tracking.

## Template

```
You are a talent development specialist. Create development plan for:

Development Context:
- Individual/Team: [TARGET]
- Current State: [CURRENT_COMPETENCIES]
- Target State: [TARGET_COMPETENCIES]
- Timeline: [DEVELOPMENT_TIMELINE]
- Resources: [AVAILABLE_RESOURCES]

Generate comprehensive development plan:

## GAP ANALYSIS

### Current State Assessment
- Current competency levels
- Strengths and capabilities
- Development areas
- Performance history
- Career aspirations

### Target State Definition
- Required competency levels
- Role requirements
- Career goals
- Organizational needs
- Timeline expectations

### Gap Identification
- Competency gaps by area
- Priority gaps
- Development difficulty
- Resource requirements
- Timeline estimates

## DEVELOPMENT STRATEGIES

### On-the-Job Learning (70%)
- Stretch assignments
- Job rotation
- Special projects
- Task force participation
- Cross-functional exposure
- Shadowing and observation

### Coaching & Mentoring (20%)
- Manager coaching
- Peer mentoring
- Executive sponsors
- Subject matter experts
- Professional coaches
- Peer learning groups

### Formal Learning (10%)
- Training courses and workshops
- Certifications and credentials
- Academic programs
- Conferences and seminars
- Online learning platforms
- Self-study materials

### Development Activities
- Action learning projects
- Communities of practice
- Book clubs and discussions
- Lunch and learns
- Job aids and tools
- Practice and simulation

## CAREER PATHWAYS

### Progression Routes
- Vertical advancement
- Lateral moves
- Cross-functional transitions
- Specialist vs. generalist tracks
- Leadership pipelines

### Succession Planning
- Critical role identification
- Successor identification
- Readiness assessment
- Development acceleration
- Risk mitigation

## TRACKING & MEASUREMENT

### Progress Monitoring
- Development milestones
- Competency reassessment
- Performance indicators
- Feedback collection
- Goal achievement

### Success Metrics
- Competency improvement
- Performance enhancement
- Career advancement
- Retention rates
- Engagement scores

## DELIVERABLES

- Individual development plans (IDPs)
- Gap analysis reports
- Learning roadmaps
- Career pathway maps
- Succession plans
- Progress dashboards

Ensure development is personalized, achievable, and aligned to goals.
```

## Usage Example
Use for creating development plans, career planning, or succession planning.
