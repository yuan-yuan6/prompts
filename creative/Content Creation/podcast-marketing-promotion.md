## MARKETING CAMPAIGN EXAMPLES

### New Episode Launch Campaign
**Timeline**: 3 days before to 3 days after release

**Pre-Launch** (3 days before):
- Teaser quote graphic on social media
- Behind-the-scenes story or video
- Email to subscribers with sneak peek
- Schedule social posts for launch day

**Launch Day**:
- Publish episode across all platforms
- Social media announcement with link
- Email blast to full list
- Guest shares to their audience
- Engage with early comments

**Post-Launch** (3 days after):
- Share listener feedback and reviews
- Create audiograms of best moments
- Follow up with non-openers
- Thank guest publicly
- Monitor and respond to engagement

### Guest Promotion Partnership
**Before Recording**:
- Share guest's work to build anticipation
- Create preview graphics and teasers
- Coordinate promotional calendar

**Recording Week**:
- Share behind-the-scenes content
- Tag guest in posts
- Build excitement for release

**Launch and Beyond**:
- Both parties share to audiences
- Cross-tag and engage
- Create shareable clips
- Continue conversation in comments

## Best Practices

1. **Be consistent** - Regular posting schedule builds audience
2. **Provide value first** - Help before you ask
3. **Engage authentically** - Real conversations, not just broadcasts
4. **Leverage guests** - Their audience becomes yours
5. **Repurpose content** - One episode = 10+ pieces of content
6. **Build email list** - Own your audience relationship
7. **Track what works** - Data drives decisions
8. **Collaborate often** - Cross-promotion amplifies reach
9. **Show personality** - Let your unique voice shine
10. **Be patient** - Growth compounds over time

## Tips for Success

- Create shareable audiograms and video clips from episodes
- Build relationships with other podcasters in your niche
- Respond to every comment and message in first 6 months
- Use Instagram Stories for behind-the-scenes content
- Start email list from day one of podcast
- Cross-promote with complementary (not competitive) shows
- Submit episodes to podcast awards and lists
- Appear as guest on other podcasts regularly
- Create a content bank of evergreen social posts
- Celebrate milestones with your community
