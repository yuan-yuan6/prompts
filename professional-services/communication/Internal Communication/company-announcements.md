## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Major Restructuring Announcement
```markdown
Company: 5,000-employee technology firm
Announcement: Division merger and 15% workforce reduction
Context: Market downturn requiring cost reduction
Challenges: Employee morale, retention of key talent
Approach: Phased communication, extensive Q&A, support resources
Timeline: 6-week implementation with weekly updates
```

### Example 2: Acquisition Communication
```markdown
Organization: Mid-size manufacturing company
Announcement: Acquisition by larger competitor
Stakeholders: 2,500 employees across 8 locations
Concerns: Job security, culture preservation, benefit changes
Strategy: Leadership visibility, frequent updates, integration planning
Focus: Opportunity messaging, career development, synergy benefits
```

### Example 3: Strategic Pivot Announcement
```markdown
Business: Software startup, 200 employees
Change: Product focus shift and market repositioning
Impact: Role changes, skill requirements, customer base
Communication: All-hands meeting, department sessions, individual discussions
Support: Retraining programs, career transition assistance
Measurement: Employee engagement, retention rates, performance metrics
```

### Example 4: Financial Performance Update
```markdown
Company: Public corporation, quarterly earnings
Performance: Mixed results with growth challenges
Audiences: Employees, investors, analysts, media
Message: Honest assessment, improvement plans, future outlook
Channels: Earnings call, internal meeting, investor presentation
Follow-up: Department briefings, manager toolkits, FAQ updates
```
