## Best Practices

1. **Establish clear communication protocols** - Set expectations for response times and channels
2. **Document everything** - Maintain written records of decisions and approvals
3. **Manage scope proactively** - Address scope creep early and transparently
4. **Set realistic timelines** - Build in buffer for revisions and unexpected issues
5. **Maintain regular contact** - Consistent updates prevent surprises and build trust
6. **Be solution-oriented** - Present options when problems arise
7. **Clarify decision authority** - Know who has final approval power
8. **Build in quality checkpoints** - Catch issues before client review
9. **Celebrate milestones** - Acknowledge progress to maintain momentum
10. **Learn from each project** - Conduct retrospectives for continuous improvement

## Tips for Success

- Create detailed project briefs to ensure alignment from the start
- Use visual tracking tools (Gantt charts, Kanban boards) for transparency
- Establish feedback templates to guide constructive client input
- Build contingency time into schedules for unexpected revisions
- Maintain calm, professional communication during challenging moments
- Provide options and recommendations, not just problems
- Use video calls for complex discussions to avoid misunderstandings
- Create client-facing preview systems (Frame.io, Vimeo Review, etc.)
- Document lessons learned for future project improvements
- Nurture client relationships beyond just project deliverables
