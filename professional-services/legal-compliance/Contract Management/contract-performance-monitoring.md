## Best Practices

1. **Define clear metrics** - Establish measurable KPIs aligned with business objectives
2. **Automate data collection** - Reduce manual effort and improve accuracy
3. **Review regularly** - Conduct scheduled performance reviews with stakeholders
4. **Act on insights** - Convert performance data into improvement actions
5. **Communicate transparently** - Share performance information with vendors
6. **Recognize excellence** - Celebrate and reward strong performance
7. **Address issues quickly** - Don't let performance problems fester
8. **Focus on value** - Measure outcomes, not just activities

## Tips for Success

- Start with critical few metrics rather than measuring everything
- Ensure data quality through validation and reconciliation
- Use visual dashboards for easy consumption of information
- Build performance discussions into regular governance meetings
- Balance lagging indicators with leading predictive metrics
- Involve vendors in defining metrics and improvement plans
- Benchmark performance against industry standards and peers
- Continuously refine metrics based on changing business needs
