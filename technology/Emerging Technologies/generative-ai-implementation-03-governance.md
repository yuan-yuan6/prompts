# Generative AI Implementation - Part 3: Implementation & Governance

## Purpose
Implement comprehensive AI safety and security controls, ensure privacy and regulatory compliance, manage deployment and organizational change, drive user adoption, and establish governance frameworks for responsible AI operations.

## Quick Start

Implement governance and deploy safely in three simple steps:

1. **Add Safety Controls** - Implement content filtering, bias detection, human review workflows
2. **Deploy with Governance** - Establish policies, audit trails, compliance monitoring
3. **Drive Adoption** - Train users, measure impact, iterate based on feedback

**Time to complete:** 4-8 weeks for governance and deployment
**Output:** Safety controls, compliance framework, deployment plan, adoption metrics

## Best Practices

1. **Build safety from day one** - Don't add governance as afterthought
2. **Implement human oversight** - Keep humans in the loop for critical decisions
3. **Test for bias continuously** - Regular testing across diverse populations
4. **Document everything** - Maintain comprehensive audit trails
5. **Train thoroughly** - Invest in user education and adoption
6. **Measure impact** - Track both technical metrics and business outcomes
7. **Communicate transparently** - Be clear about AI use and limitations
8. **Iterate based on feedback** - Continuously improve based on user input

## Customization Options

### By Safety Approach
- **Risk-Averse:** Extensive controls, human review, conservative deployment
- **Balanced:** Reasonable controls, selective review, phased deployment
- **Innovation-Focused:** Minimum viable controls, rapid iteration, experimental
- **Mission-Critical:** Maximum safety, redundant controls, extensive validation

### By Governance Model
- **Centralized:** Single AI governance team, standardized policies
- **Federated:** Department-level governance, shared standards
- **Hybrid:** Central oversight with distributed execution
- **Decentralized:** Business unit autonomy with light coordination

### By Deployment Speed
- **Rapid:** 4-8 week pilot to production
- **Standard:** 3-6 month phased rollout
- **Conservative:** 6-12 month careful deployment
- **Experimental:** Ongoing pilots with selective scaling
