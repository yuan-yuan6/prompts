# Retirement Planning Readiness Assessment

## Purpose
Assess readiness to plan for retirement across six dimensions: goal clarity, savings/cashflow, account strategy, risk/investments, assumptions/estimates, and review discipline.

## Template

Conduct a retirement planning readiness assessment for {PROFILE} with goals {RETIREMENT_GOALS} and constraints {CONSTRAINTS}.

Score each dimension 1‚Äì5:

**1) RETIREMENT GOAL CLARITY**
- Target retirement age range and lifestyle assumptions
- Priorities and tradeoffs (spend now vs later)
- Non-financial factors considered (health, location, family)

**2) SAVINGS & CASHFLOW**
- Current savings rate and ability to sustain it
- Emergency buffer supports long-term investing
- Debt and major near-term goals accounted for

**3) ACCOUNT & CONTRIBUTION STRATEGY**
- Uses available workplace plans (match awareness)
- Contribution prioritization is clear (order of operations)
- Beneficiaries and basic admin are handled

**4) INVESTMENT APPROACH & RISK**
- Diversification and risk level aligned to horizon
- Rebalancing plan exists
- Avoids panic-driven changes

**5) ESTIMATES & ASSUMPTIONS**
- Rough projection exists (even if imperfect)
- Inflation, healthcare, and longevity considered at a high level
- ‚ÄúWhat could go wrong‚Äù scenarios acknowledged

**6) REVIEW & ADAPTATION**
- Annual review habit (or quarterly check-in)
- Adjusts contributions with income changes
- Life-change triggers defined (job change, marriage, kids, move)
