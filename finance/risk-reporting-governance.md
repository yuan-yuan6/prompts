# Risk Reporting, Governance & Regulatory Compliance

## Purpose
Design comprehensive risk reporting and regulatory frameworks covering Basel implementation, stress testing, reporting, examination, and compliance.

## Template

```
You are a regulatory risk specialist. Create detailed compliance framework based on:

Regulatory Context:
- Primary Regulations: [BASEL_III_CCAR_STRESS_TESTING]
- Jurisdiction: [DOMESTIC_INTERNATIONAL_PRESENCE]
- Examination Status: [REGULATORY_RATING_AND_HISTORY]
- Reporting Requirements: [MANDATORY_SUBMISSIONS]

Generate comprehensive regulatory compliance covering:

## 5. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE AND EXAMINATION

### 5.1 Regulatory Framework and Requirements
- Basel III capital and liquidity requirements
- Common equity tier 1 and capital ratios
- Capital conservation and countercyclical buffers
- Leverage ratio and supplementary measures
- Liquidity coverage ratio (LCR)
- Net stable funding ratio (NSFR)
- Large exposure and concentration limits
- Market risk capital and trading book
- Operational risk capital measurement

### 5.2 Stress Testing and Capital Planning
- Comprehensive Capital Analysis and Review (CCAR)
- Dodd-Frank Act Stress Testing (DFAST)
- Internal stress testing and capital planning
- Supervisory stress testing scenarios
- Capital distribution and dividend restrictions
- Recovery and resolution planning
- Living will and orderly liquidation
- International coordination

### 5.3 Regulatory Reporting and Disclosure
- Call reports and regulatory filings
- Capital adequacy and risk-weighted assets
- Liquidity and funding risk disclosure
- Market risk and trading book capital
- Operational risk and loss data
- Credit risk and loan loss provisions
- Pillar 3 public disclosures
- Quarterly and annual reporting

### 5.4 Examination and Supervision
- Safety and soundness examinations
- Consumer compliance and fair lending
- Information technology and cybersecurity reviews
- Anti-money laundering and BSA exams
- Capital adequacy and risk management assessment
- Asset quality and credit administration
- Earnings and profitability analysis
- Management and board oversight evaluation

### 5.5 Compliance Monitoring and Testing
- Compliance risk assessment
- Regulatory requirement mapping
- Control assessment and effectiveness
- Gap analysis and remediation
- Independent testing and validation
- Issue identification and root cause analysis
- Regulatory change management
- Impact assessment and implementation

## 6.3 RISK CULTURE AND LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT

### Risk Leadership and Capability Building
- Risk management certification and training
- Leadership development and succession planning
- Cross-functional rotation and experience
- Industry knowledge and best practices
- Regulatory relationship building
- Innovation and technology adoption
- Change management capabilities
- Performance management and accountability

### Risk Culture Transformation
- Culture assessment and baseline
- Behavioral change and reinforcement
- Communication and engagement strategy
- Training and awareness programs
- Performance measurement and incentives
- Recognition and reward systems
- Leadership commitment and modeling
- Continuous feedback and improvement

### Innovation and Continuous Improvement
- Industry benchmark and peer analysis
- Academic research and thought leadership
- Regulatory guidance and expectations
- Technology and methodology advancement
- International standards and frameworks
- Professional associations and networks
- Internal innovation and idea generation
- Change management and transformation
```

## Variables
- `[BASEL_III_CCAR_STRESS_TESTING]`: Primary regulations
- `[DOMESTIC_INTERNATIONAL_PRESENCE]`: Jurisdiction
- `[REGULATORY_RATING_AND_HISTORY]`: Examination status
- `[MANDATORY_SUBMISSIONS]`: Reporting requirements

## Best Practices
1. **Comply proactively** - Stay ahead of regulatory requirements
2. **Report accurately** - Ensure data quality and timeliness
3. **Prepare continuously** - Maintain examination readiness
4. **Build relationships** - Engage with regulators constructively
5. **Improve continuously** - Learn from feedback and evolve
