## Best Practices
- Vary instructional strategies
- Engage multiple learning styles
- Use authentic, relevant content
- Incorporate technology purposefully
- Provide hands-on experiences
- Encourage collaboration
- Connect to student interests
- Balance teacher-directed and student-centered activities
