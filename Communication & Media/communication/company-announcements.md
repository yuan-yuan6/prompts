# Company Announcements & All-Hands Readiness Assessment

## Purpose
Assess readiness to deliver **clear, credible, and aligned company-wide announcements** that inform, inspire, and drive action. Use this to diagnose gaps in message discipline, cascade execution, and feedback integration.

## Template

Conduct a company announcements & all-hands readiness assessment for {ANNOUNCEMENT_CONTEXT}.

Score each dimension **1â€“5** (1 = ad hoc, 5 = optimized). Ground findings in observable signals (employee understanding, trust scores, rumor frequency, action follow-through).

**1) MESSAGE CLARITY & FRAMING**
- Headline and "why this matters" are upfront (SCQA: Situation, Complication, Question, Answer)
- Key messages are simple, memorable, and consistent across channels
- Assumptions, tradeoffs, and "what's not changing" are stated explicitly
- Jargon and ambiguity are minimized for all audience levels

**2) AUDIENCE SEGMENTATION & TAILORING**
- Messaging is segmented by impact (all-company vs. function-specific)
- Leaders, managers, and front-line employees get appropriate detail
- Remote, distributed, and shift workers receive equitable access
- Multilingual and accessibility needs are addressed

**3) CASCADE & AMPLIFICATION DISCIPLINE**
- Leaders are briefed 24â€“48 hours before the announcement to prepare
- Managers receive talking points, FAQs, and guidance for team conversations
- Cascade timeline is tight (24â€“48 hours) to prevent rumor spread
- Executive visibility and reinforcement happen consistently post-announcement

**4) CHANNEL STRATEGY & DELIVERY**
- Channel mix matches audience preference (live, video, email, intranet, Slack)
- Timing avoids major conflicts (holidays, end-of-quarter, competitive events)
- Format is appropriate for message sensitivity and interaction needs
- All-hands or town halls include Q&A and are recorded for asynchronous access

**5) TWO-WAY DIALOGUE & FEEDBACK**
- Q&A is structured with pre-submitted and live questions
- Anonymous feedback channels exist and are monitored
- Follow-up on unanswered questions happens within 48 hours
- Feedback themes inform message iteration and next communications

**6) FOLLOW-THROUGH & MEASUREMENT**
- Action items have owners, timelines, and are tracked
- Progress updates are scheduled and delivered on time
- Effectiveness is measured (reach, comprehension, sentiment, behavior change)
- Lessons learned inform future announcement improvements
