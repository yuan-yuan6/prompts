## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Fashion Retailer
```
Business: Multi-brand fashion retailer
Members: 2M active
Tiers: 4 levels (Style, Elite, VIP, Icon)
Rewards: Points, exclusive access, personal styling
Technology: Salesforce Loyalty Management
Personalization: AI-driven recommendations
Partners: Beauty brands, restaurants
ROI: 3.5x program investment
```

### Example 2: Grocery Chain
```
Business: Regional grocery chain
Members: 5M households
Program: Points-based with fuel rewards
Digital Integration: Mobile app with coupons
Personalization: Purchase-based offers
Partners: Gas stations, pharmacies
Gamification: Weekly challenges
Retention: 85% annual
```

### Example 3: Travel & Hospitality
```
Business: Hotel chain
Members: 10M global
Tiers: 5 elite levels
Benefits: Free nights, upgrades, experiences
Partners: Airlines, car rentals, dining
Technology: Custom platform
Mobile: Digital key, mobile check-in
LTV Increase: 40%
```
