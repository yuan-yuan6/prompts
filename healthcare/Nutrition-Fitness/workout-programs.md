# Workout Programs Template

## Overview
This comprehensive template assists fitness professionals, healthcare providers, and individuals in designing evidence-based, progressive workout programs that optimize physical performance, health outcomes, and injury prevention while accommodating individual capabilities and goals.

## Quick Start

**Get started in 3 steps:**

1. **Assess Starting Point** - Complete Section 1 (Client Assessment) with physical measurements, fitness test results, medical history, and movement screening. Document any limitations or injury history upfront.

2. **Set Program Framework** - Use Section 2 (Program Design) to establish your periodization model, training frequency, and session duration. Choose appropriate training split (Full Body for beginners, Upper/Lower for intermediate).

3. **Design Week 1** - Build your first week using Section 3 (Weekly Training Structure) and Section 4 (Exercise Prescription). Start conservative: 60-70% intensity, 2-3 sets, 8-12 reps for main lifts.

**Pro Tip:** Most beginners succeed with 3-day full-body programs using compound movements (squat, hinge, push, pull). Master form first, then progressively add weight by 2.5-5% weekly as outlined in Section 5 (Progressive Overload).

## Template Structure

### 1. Client Assessment and Fitness Profiling

**Comprehensive Fitness Assessment Framework:**
```
As a [FITNESS_PROFESSIONAL_ROLE] with [CERTIFICATION_TYPE] working in [FACILITY_TYPE] with [YEARS_EXPERIENCE] years of experience, I am designing a workout program for [CLIENT_AGE]-year-old [CLIENT_GENDER] [CLIENT_ETHNICITY] with [PRIMARY_FITNESS_GOAL].

Physical Assessment Data: Height [HEIGHT_MEASUREMENT], Weight [CURRENT_WEIGHT], BMI [BMI_CALCULATION], Body fat percentage [BODY_FAT_PERCENTAGE], Muscle mass [MUSCLE_MASS_MEASUREMENT], Bone density [BONE_DENSITY_SCORE].

Cardiovascular Assessment: Resting heart rate [RESTING_HR] BPM, Blood pressure [BP_READING], VO2 max [VO2_MAX_ESTIMATE], Submaximal fitness test [FITNESS_TEST_RESULT], Recovery heart rate [RECOVERY_HR_TIME].

Musculoskeletal Assessment:
- Flexibility: [FLEXIBILITY_TEST_RESULTS]
- Core stability: [CORE_STABILITY_ASSESSMENT]
- Balance: [BALANCE_TEST_SCORES]
- Posture: [POSTURAL_ASSESSMENT]
- Movement patterns: [MOVEMENT_SCREEN_RESULTS]
- Joint mobility: [MOBILITY_LIMITATIONS]

### Strength Baseline
- Upper body: Bench press [BENCH_PRESS_1RM], Pull-ups [PULLUP_MAX], Shoulder press [SHOULDER_PRESS_1RM]
- Lower body: Squat [SQUAT_1RM], Deadlift [DEADLIFT_1RM], Single leg press [LEG_PRESS_1RM]
- Core: Plank hold [PLANK_TIME], Dead bug [DEAD_BUG_REPS]
- Grip strength: [GRIP_STRENGTH_MEASUREMENT]

Medical History: [PRIMARY_MEDICAL_CONDITIONS], [ORTHOPEDIC_INJURIES], [CARDIOVASCULAR_CONDITIONS], [MEDICATION_LIST], [PHYSICAL_LIMITATIONS], [PAIN_AREAS], [SURGERY_HISTORY], [CHRONIC_CONDITIONS].

Exercise History: Previous experience [EXERCISE_EXPERIENCE_LEVEL], Preferred activities [PREFERRED_ACTIVITIES], Injuries from exercise [EXERCISE_INJURY_HISTORY], Current activity level [CURRENT_ACTIVITY_FREQUENCY], Sports background [SPORTS_BACKGROUND].

Lifestyle Factors: Work schedule [WORK_SCHEDULE_TYPE], Available workout days [AVAILABLE_DAYS_PER_WEEK], Session duration preference [PREFERRED_SESSION_LENGTH], Equipment access [EQUIPMENT_AVAILABILITY], Budget considerations [BUDGET_CONSTRAINTS].

Psychological Factors: Motivation level [MOTIVATION_ASSESSMENT], Exercise barriers [BARRIER_IDENTIFICATION], Support system [SUPPORT_SYSTEM_STRENGTH], Past program adherence [ADHERENCE_HISTORY], Self-efficacy [CONFIDENCE_LEVEL].

Goal Specifications: Primary goal [PRIMARY_GOAL_SPECIFIC], Secondary goals [SECONDARY_GOALS_LIST], Timeline [GOAL_TIMELINE], Success metrics [SUCCESS_MEASUREMENTS], Lifestyle integration [INTEGRATION_PRIORITIES].
```

```
As a Certified Personal Trainer with ACSM CPT working in Commercial Gym with 6 years of experience, I am designing a workout program for 35-year-old Female Caucasian with General fitness and strength building.

### Program Overview
Program name: Foundation Strength Builder
Duration: 12 weeks
Phases: Adaptation, Strength Building, Consolidation
Training frequency: 3 sessions per week
Session duration: 45 minutes

Training Split: Full Body
Monday: Full Body Strength
- Primary muscle groups: All major muscle groups
- Session type: Compound movement focus
- Duration: 45 minutes
- Intensity level: Moderate (65-75% effort)

Exercise 1: Goblet Squat
- Sets: 3 x Reps: 10-12
- Load: 15-25 lbs dumbbell
- Rest: 60-90 seconds
- Tempo: 3-1-2-1 (controlled descent, pause, explosive up, pause)
- Progression: Add 2.5-5 lbs every 2 weeks
```
