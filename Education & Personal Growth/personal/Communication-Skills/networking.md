# Networking Readiness Assessment

## Purpose
Assess readiness to build and maintain effective professional networks across six dimensions: Goal Clarity & Targeting, Value Proposition, Approach & Authenticity, Communication Skills, Follow-Up Systems, and Relationship Nurturing. This framework identifies networking capability gaps, strengthens connection strategies, and creates sustainable relationship-building practices.

## Template

Assess networking readiness for {NETWORKING_CONTEXT} targeting {TARGET_CONNECTIONS} to achieve {GOALS}.

Evaluate readiness across six dimensions, scoring each 1-5:

**1. GOAL CLARITY & TARGETING**
- Specific networking objectives and success definition
- Target connection identification (who, not just "anyone")
- Prioritization of high-value vs broad connections
- Timeline and milestone definition

**2. VALUE PROPOSITION**
- Clarity on what you offer to connections
- Expertise, resources, or access you bring
- Help you can provide without expecting immediate return
- Differentiation from others seeking similar connections

**3. APPROACH & AUTHENTICITY**
- Genuine relationship-seeking vs transactional mindset
- Conversation starters and icebreakers prepared
- Comfort with approaching new people
- Balance between confidence and humility

**4. COMMUNICATION SKILLS**
- Compelling self-introduction (2-sentence pitch)
- Active listening and genuine curiosity
- Meaningful question-asking ability
- Reading social cues and adapting

**5. FOLLOW-UP SYSTEMS**
- Connection tracking method (CRM, spreadsheet, tool)
- Follow-up timing and cadence plan
- Personalization capability at scale
- Commitment tracking (promised intros, resources shared)

**6. RELATIONSHIP NURTURING**
- Ongoing value provision strategies
- Visibility maintenance without being pushy
- Long-term relationship investment mindset
- Reciprocity and giving-first approach

## Maturity Scale

Use this scale for dimension scoring:

**1.0-1.9 (Passive):** No clear goals, transactional mindset, poor follow-up, relationships fade quickly

**2.0-2.9 (Reactive):** Basic goals, occasional outreach, inconsistent follow-up, surface-level relationships

**3.0-3.9 (Active):** Clear goals, regular outreach, systematic follow-up, developing meaningful connections

**4.0-4.9 (Strategic):** Well-defined targets, value-first approach, sophisticated tracking, strong relationship depth

**5.0 (Exemplary):** Masterful networking with rich ecosystem, effortless value exchange, connections actively seek you out
