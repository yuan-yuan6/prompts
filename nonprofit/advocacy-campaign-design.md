# Advocacy Campaign Design & Execution

## Purpose
Framework for designing and executing advocacy campaigns, grassroots mobilization, coalition building, policy change strategies, and measuring advocacy impact.

## Quick Start

### Minimal Example
```
Design advocacy campaign for healthcare access including research and messaging, coalition of 30 organizations, grassroots mobilization of 10,000 supporters, legislative strategy, media campaign, and policy win measurement.
```

### Basic 3-Step Workflow
1. **Campaign Design** - Goals, targets, strategy, tactics (4-6 weeks)
2. **Mobilization** - Coalition, grassroots, media, lobbying (3-12 months)
3. **Victory & Evaluation** - Policy change, lessons learned (ongoing)

---

## Key Components

- Policy research and analysis
- Theory of change for advocacy
- Target identification (decision makers)
- Coalition building and management
- Message development and testing
- Grassroots mobilization tactics
- Media and communications strategy
- Legislative and regulatory strategy
- Campaign metrics and evaluation
