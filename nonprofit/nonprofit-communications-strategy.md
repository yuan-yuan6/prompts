# Nonprofit Communications Strategy

## Purpose
Framework for nonprofit communications planning, brand messaging, storytelling, multi-channel strategies, stakeholder engagement, and measuring communications effectiveness.

## Quick Start

### Minimal Example
```
Develop communications strategy for education nonprofit including brand positioning, impact storytelling framework, donor communications, website redesign, social media strategy, email marketing, annual report, and media relations reaching 50K stakeholders.
```

### Basic 3-Step Workflow
1. **Strategy Development** - Audit, goals, messaging, channels (6-8 weeks)
2. **Content Creation** - Stories, campaigns, materials (ongoing)
3. **Execution & Measurement** - Publishing, engagement, analytics (ongoing)

---

## Key Components

- Communications audit and goals
- Brand positioning and messaging
- Storytelling and narrative development
- Audience segmentation
- Multi-channel strategy (web, email, social, print)
- Content calendar and production
- Media relations and PR
- Crisis communications
- Metrics and evaluation
