# Telemedicine Compliance & Regulatory Framework

## Purpose
Design comprehensive telemedicine compliance frameworks covering regulatory requirements, licensing, reimbursement, and legal considerations.

## Template

```
You are a telemedicine compliance specialist. Create detailed compliance framework based on:

Regulatory Context:
- Jurisdiction: [PRIMARY_OPERATING_STATES]
- License Requirements: [PROVIDER_LICENSING_NEEDS]
- Reimbursement: [PAYER_MIX_AND_COVERAGE]

Generate comprehensive compliance guidance covering:

## 4. COMPLIANCE AND REGULATORY FRAMEWORK

### 4.1 Regulatory Compliance
- State licensure requirements and interstate practice
- DEA registration for controlled substances
- HIPAA privacy and security compliance
- Informed consent requirements
- Prescribing regulations and restrictions
- Medical board standards and guidelines
- Malpractice insurance requirements
- Quality and safety regulations

### 4.2 Credentialing and Privileging
- Telemedicine-specific credentialing criteria
- Primary source verification requirements
- Privileging by proxy arrangements
- Interstate credentialing compacts
- Ongoing professional practice evaluation
- Peer review and quality monitoring
- Credential renewal and maintenance
- Disciplinary action and reporting

### 4.3 Consent and Patient Rights
- Informed consent for telemedicine
- Privacy and security disclosures
- Video recording and storage policies
- Patient rights and responsibilities
- Language access and interpretation
- Disability accommodation requirements
- Minor and guardian consent
- Research and data use authorization

### 4.4 Reimbursement and Billing
- Medicare telemedicine coverage and rules
- Medicaid state-specific policies
- Commercial payer requirements
- Originating site and distant site rules
- CPT code selection and modifiers
- Place of service coding
- Documentation requirements
- Audit preparation and response

### 4.5 Legal and Liability Considerations
- Standard of care requirements
- Professional liability coverage
- Malpractice risk management
- Emergency response obligations
- Duty to warn and protect
- Abandonment prevention
- Record retention requirements
- Litigation and discovery procedures
```

## Variables
- `[PRIMARY_OPERATING_STATES]`: Operating jurisdictions
- `[PROVIDER_LICENSING_NEEDS]`: Licensing requirements
- `[PAYER_MIX_AND_COVERAGE]`: Insurance coverage

## Best Practices
1. **Ensure compliance** - Follow all state and federal regulations
2. **Maintain proper licensing** - Track multi-state requirements
3. **Obtain informed consent** - Document patient agreement
4. **Code accurately** - Follow payer-specific billing rules
5. **Manage liability** - Maintain appropriate insurance coverage
