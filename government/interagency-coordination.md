# Interagency Coordination & Collaboration

## Purpose
Framework for coordinating across government agencies, establishing partnerships, managing multi-jurisdictional initiatives, information sharing, and collaborative problem-solving for complex public challenges.

## Quick Start

### Minimal Example
```
Coordinate homelessness response across 5 city departments, county services, state programs, and federal funding agencies. Establish governance structure, data sharing agreements, coordinated service delivery, and unified accountability metrics.
```

### Basic 3-Step Workflow
1. **Partnership Formation** - Stakeholder engagement, MOU development (1-2 months)
2. **Infrastructure Setup** - Governance, data sharing, protocols (2-3 months)
3. **Collaborative Operations** - Coordinated delivery, continuous improvement (ongoing)

---

## Key Components

- Stakeholder mapping and engagement
- Governance structures (lead agency, joint authority)
- Memoranda of Understanding (MOUs)
- Data sharing agreements and platforms
- Coordinated service delivery
- Resource pooling and leveraging
- Joint accountability and metrics
- Conflict resolution mechanisms
