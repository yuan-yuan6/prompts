## Overview

## Customization Options

### 1. Client Type Specializations
- **Individual Investors**: Personal financial goals, tax optimization, and retirement planning focus
- **High Net Worth**: Alternative investments, tax efficiency, and estate planning integration
- **Institutional**: Long-term horizons, governance requirements, and spending policy alignment
- **Retirement Plans**: ERISA compliance, participant education, and lifecycle approaches
- **Endowments/Foundations**: Perpetual time horizons, spending policies, and mission alignment

### 2. Risk Profile Adaptations
- **Conservative**: Capital preservation focus, income generation, and lower volatility tolerance
- **Moderate**: Balanced growth and income, moderate risk tolerance, and diversification emphasis
- **Aggressive**: Growth maximization, higher risk tolerance, and longer time horizons
- **Risk Parity**: Equal risk contribution, factor-based approach, and volatility targeting
- **Tactical**: Active allocation adjustments, market timing elements, and flexibility emphasis

### 3. Investment Approach Variations
- **Passive/Index**: Low-cost index funds, market efficiency beliefs, and minimal active management
- **Active Management**: Security selection focus, manager research, and alpha generation pursuit
