## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Healthcare Policy Reform
```
Agency: Department of Health
Policy Area: Universal healthcare access
Population: 10 million residents
Budget: $5 billion over 5 years
Implementation: Phased rollout over 3 years
Outcomes: 95% coverage, 30% cost reduction
Key Features: Public option, subsidized premiums
```

### Example 2: Climate Action Policy
```
Agency: Environmental Protection Agency
Policy Area: Carbon emissions reduction
Target: 50% reduction by 2030
Budget: $2 billion annual
Mechanisms: Carbon tax, renewable incentives
Stakeholders: Industry, utilities, citizens
Monitoring: Real-time emissions tracking
```

### Example 3: Education Reform Initiative
```

Budget: $1 billion over 4 years
Programs: Teacher training, technology access
Metrics: Test scores, graduation rates
Implementation: District-by-district rollout
```
