# Volunteer Recruitment & Management

## Purpose
Framework for volunteer program development, recruitment strategies, screening and onboarding, engagement activities, retention programs, and volunteer impact measurement.

## Quick Start

### Minimal Example
```
Build volunteer program for food bank recruiting 500 volunteers annually. Include role definitions, online application system, background checks, orientation training, scheduling platform, recognition program, and retention strategies achieving 60%+ annual retention.
```

### Basic 3-Step Workflow
1. **Program Design** - Roles, policies, infrastructure (4-6 weeks)
2. **Recruitment & Onboarding** - Marketing, screening, training (ongoing)
3. **Engagement & Retention** - Management, recognition, development (ongoing)

---

## Key Components

- Volunteer needs assessment
- Role descriptions and expectations
- Recruitment marketing strategies
- Application and screening process
- Orientation and training programs
- Scheduling and management systems
- Supervision and support
- Recognition and retention programs
- Impact measurement
