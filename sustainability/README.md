# Sustainability & ESG Templates

Comprehensive templates for sustainability strategy, ESG reporting, climate action, circular economy, and social impact programs across all industries.

## Overview

This category provides templates for organizations building sustainability programs, implementing ESG frameworks, addressing climate change, and creating social impact. Templates cover strategy development, measurement, reporting, and operational implementation.

## Why Sustainability Matters in 2025

- **Investor Expectations:** ESG performance affects valuations and access to capital
- **Regulatory Requirements:** CSRD, TCFD, SEC climate disclosure rules
- **Customer Demand:** Consumers increasingly choose sustainable brands
- **Talent Attraction:** Employees want to work for responsible companies
- **Risk Management:** Climate and social risks impact business continuity
- **Competitive Advantage:** Sustainability as differentiation
