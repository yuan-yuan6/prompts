## Key Considerations Across All Parts

### Strategic Considerations
1. **Business Value First** - Focus on high-impact use cases
2. **Start Small, Scale Fast** - Begin with pilots, expand quickly
3. **Human-AI Collaboration** - Augment, don't replace humans
4. **ROI Measurement** - Track tangible business benefits

### Technical Considerations
1. **Model Selection** - Choose appropriate model for use case
2. **Prompt Engineering** - Invest in effective prompting
3. **Performance Optimization** - Balance quality, speed, cost
4. **Integration** - Connect with existing systems and workflows

### Governance Considerations
1. **Responsible AI** - Build ethics and safety from start
2. **Compliance** - Ensure regulatory adherence
3. **Risk Management** - Identify and mitigate AI-specific risks
4. **Transparency** - Maintain explainability and auditability

## Best Practices for Using This Framework

1. **Define clear objectives** - Know what you want to achieve with AI
2. **Assess readiness** - Evaluate organizational capabilities and gaps
3. **Select appropriate models** - Match models to use cases and requirements
4. **Implement safety controls** - Build responsible AI from the start
5. **Train your team** - Develop AI literacy across the organization
6. **Measure continuously** - Track performance, quality, and business impact
7. **Iterate rapidly** - Use agile approaches for AI development
8. **Govern proactively** - Establish clear policies and oversight
