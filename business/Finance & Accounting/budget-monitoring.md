- `[ACCEPTABLE_DEVIATION_LIMITS]`: Variance threshold levels
- `[BUDGET_APPROVAL_WORKFLOW]`: Approval process requirements
- `[TRACKING_MECHANISMS]`: Monitoring system requirements
