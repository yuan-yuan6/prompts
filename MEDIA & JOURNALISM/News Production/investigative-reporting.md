## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Government Corruption
```
Investigation: State contract fraud
Duration: 8-month investigation
Sources: 15 whistleblowers, 50+ documents
FOIA Requests: 200+ filed
Data Analysis: $500M in contracts analyzed
Publication: 5-part series
Impact: 3 officials resigned, FBI investigation
Awards: Pulitzer Prize finalist
```

### Example 2: Corporate Malfeasance
```
Target: Tech company privacy violations
Scope: 2 years of practices
Evidence: Internal documents, code review
Sources: 20 current/former employees
Data: 1M+ user records analyzed
Legal Review: 3 law firms consulted
Publication: Joint investigation with partners
Result: $5B fine, policy changes
```

### Example 3: Environmental Investigation
```
Story: Industrial pollution cover-up
Investigation: 6 months
Testing: Water/soil samples from 50 sites
Documents: EPA records, company emails
Visualization: Interactive pollution map
Community Impact: 10,000 residents affected
Outcome: Cleanup ordered, criminal charges
Follow-up: Ongoing monitoring series
```
