# Meeting Facilitation & Execution

## Purpose
Design comprehensive meeting facilitation frameworks covering opening, time management, participation engagement, decision-making, and closing.

## Template

```
You are a meeting facilitation specialist. Create detailed facilitation guidance based on:

Facilitation Context:
- Meeting Size: [NUMBER_OF_PARTICIPANTS]
- Meeting Style: [FORMAL_INFORMAL_VIRTUAL_HYBRID]
- Complexity: [DISCUSSION_COMPLEXITY_LEVEL]
- Stakes: [DECISION_IMPORTANCE_LEVEL]

Generate comprehensive facilitation guidance covering:

## 2. MEETING FACILITATION AND EXECUTION

### 2.1 Meeting Opening
- Punctual start and welcome
- Agenda review and time check
- Objectives and outcomes reminder
- Ground rules and norms
- Participant introductions (if needed)
- Technology check and setup
- Housekeeping and logistics
- Engagement and energy setting

### 2.2 Time Management
- Agenda pacing and monitoring
- Time warnings and transitions
- Parking lot for off-topic items
- Efficient discussion techniques
- Timeboxing and time limits
- Schedule adjustment as needed
- Break management
- Respectful time closure

### 2.3 Participation Management
- Balanced participation encouragement
- Drawing out quiet participants
- Managing dominant speakers
- Round-robin and structured input
- Small group breakouts
- Polling and voting techniques
- Active listening demonstration
- Psychological safety creation

### 2.4 Decision-Making and Consensus
- Decision-making process clarity
- Options presentation and discussion
- Pros and cons exploration
- Stakeholder perspective inclusion
- Consensus building techniques
- Voting and decision recording
- Action item assignment
- Next steps clarification
```

## Variables
- `[NUMBER_OF_PARTICIPANTS]`: Meeting size
- `[FORMAL_INFORMAL_VIRTUAL_HYBRID]`: Meeting style
- `[DISCUSSION_COMPLEXITY_LEVEL]`: Complexity
- `[DECISION_IMPORTANCE_LEVEL]`: Stakes

## Best Practices
1. **Start on time** - Respect everyone's schedule
2. **Manage time actively** - Keep to agenda
3. **Engage everyone** - Draw out all perspectives
4. **Make decisions** - Don't leave without outcomes
5. **End with clarity** - Confirm actions and next steps
