```markdown
Situation: New manager, 5 direct reports
Challenge: Uncomfortable giving negative feedback
Team dynamic: Previously peers, now supervisor
Goal: Build credibility while maintaining relationships
Support needed: Templates, coaching, practice
```

### Example 2: Performance Improvement Plan
```markdown
Recipient: Senior developer, 3 years tenure
Issues: Missing deadlines, quality problems
Previous feedback: Informal warnings ineffective
Next steps: Formal PIP required
Risk: Valuable technical knowledge, flight risk
```

### Example 3: High Performer Recognition
```markdown
Context: Top salesperson exceeding targets
Challenge: Retention risk, competitor interest
Current recognition: Standard bonus only
Objective: Meaningful appreciation, career growth
Considerations: Team morale, fairness perception
```
