## Usage Examples

### Example 1: High Net Worth Individual Portfolio
```
Client: Individual investor, $50M portfolio
Risk Profile: Moderate-aggressive
Investment Horizon: 20+ years
Goals: Wealth preservation, growth, tax efficiency
Asset Mix: 60% equities, 25% fixed income, 15% alternatives
Expected Return: 8-10% annually
```

### Example 2: Institutional Pension Fund
```
Client: Corporate pension fund, $2B assets
Risk Profile: Conservative
Investment Horizon: Long-term (30+ years)
Goals: Meet pension obligations, stable returns
Asset Mix: 45% equities, 35% bonds, 20% alternatives
Expected Return: 6-8% annually
```

### Example 3: Family Office Multi-Generational Wealth
```
Client: Multi-generational family, $500M assets
Risk Profile: Balanced
Investment Horizon: Perpetual
Goals: Capital preservation, growth, ESG impact
Asset Mix: 50% equities, 30% fixed income, 20% alternatives
Expected Return: 7-9% annually
```
