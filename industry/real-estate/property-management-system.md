# Property Management & Real Estate Operations Framework

## Purpose
Comprehensive framework for managing real estate properties including tenant relations, maintenance operations, financial management, lease administration, compliance tracking, and portfolio optimization for residential and commercial properties.

## Template

Manage property portfolio of [PROPERTY_COUNT] properties with [UNIT_COUNT] units, [SQUARE_FOOTAGE] total sq ft, [TENANT_COUNT] tenants, achieving [OCCUPANCY_RATE]% occupancy, [COLLECTION_RATE]% rent collection, [MAINTENANCE_RESPONSE] response time, [NOI_TARGET] NOI, with [TENANT_SATISFACTION] satisfaction score.

### 1. Portfolio Overview & Management

| **Property Type** | **Units/Properties** | **Square Footage** | **Occupancy Rate** | **Average Rent** | **Annual Revenue** |
|------------------|---------------------|-------------------|-------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Residential Multi-Family | [MULTI_UNITS] | [MULTI_SQFT] | [MULTI_OCCUPANCY]% | $[MULTI_RENT] | $[MULTI_REVENUE] |
| Single Family Homes | [SFH_UNITS] | [SFH_SQFT] | [SFH_OCCUPANCY]% | $[SFH_RENT] | $[SFH_REVENUE] |
| Commercial Office | [OFFICE_UNITS] | [OFFICE_SQFT] | [OFFICE_OCCUPANCY]% | $[OFFICE_RENT]/sqft | $[OFFICE_REVENUE] |
| Retail Properties | [RETAIL_UNITS] | [RETAIL_SQFT] | [RETAIL_OCCUPANCY]% | $[RETAIL_RENT]/sqft | $[RETAIL_REVENUE] |
| Industrial/Warehouse | [INDUSTRIAL_UNITS] | [INDUSTRIAL_SQFT] | [INDUSTRIAL_OCCUPANCY]% | $[INDUSTRIAL_RENT]/sqft | $[INDUSTRIAL_REVENUE] |
| Mixed-Use Properties | [MIXED_UNITS] | [MIXED_SQFT] | [MIXED_OCCUPANCY]% | $[MIXED_RENT] | $[MIXED_REVENUE] |
