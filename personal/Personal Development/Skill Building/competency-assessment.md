- **Healthcare:** Clinical competencies, patient care, medical knowledge, safety protocols
- **Finance:** Risk management, analytical skills, regulatory knowledge, client management
- **Education:** Teaching skills, subject expertise, student engagement, assessment methods
- **Manufacturing:** Technical skills, safety competencies, quality control, process improvement

### Option 2: Assessment Purpose Customization
Tailor for different assessment objectives:
- **Hiring & Selection:** Job fit, candidate evaluation, selection criteria, predictive validity
- **Performance Management:** Annual reviews, goal setting, development planning, career progression
- **Development Planning:** Skill gaps, learning needs, growth opportunities, career paths
- **Succession Planning:** Leadership potential, readiness assessment, development priorities
- **Certification & Licensing:** Professional standards, competency validation, regulatory compliance

### Option 3: Organizational Scale Adaptation
Adjust complexity based on organization size:
- **Small Business:** Simple assessments, cost-effective methods, basic analytics
- **Mid-Market:** Balanced approach, moderate complexity, growth scalability
- **Enterprise:** Comprehensive systems, advanced analytics, sophisticated integration
- **Multi-National:** Global standards, cultural adaptation, cross-border consistency

### Option 4: Technology Integration Level
Customize based on technological sophistication:
- **Basic Digital:** Online surveys, simple platforms, basic reporting
- **Advanced Digital:** AI-powered assessment, adaptive testing, predictive analytics
- **Emerging Technology:** VR/AR assessment, biometric feedback, blockchain credentials
- **Hybrid Approach:** Mix of digital and traditional methods, flexible deployment

### Option 5: Assessment Maturity Level
Scale based on assessment program maturity:
- **Starting:** Basic frameworks, simple methods, foundational capabilities
- **Developing:** Structured approaches, multiple methods, improved analytics
- **Advanced:** Sophisticated systems, comprehensive integration, predictive capabilities
- **Leading Edge:** Innovation adoption, research integration, continuous evolution
