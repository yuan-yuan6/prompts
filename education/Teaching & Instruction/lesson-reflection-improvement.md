## Reflection Questions
1. Did all students achieve the learning objectives?
2. What evidence shows students learned?
3. Were differentiation strategies effective?
4. Did assessments accurately measure learning?
5. How engaged were students?
6. What would I change next time?
7. What professional learning do I need?
8. How can I build on this lesson?

## Best Practices
- Reflect after every lesson
- Use data to inform reflection
- Be honest about what didn't work
- Focus on student learning, not just teaching
- Seek feedback from colleagues
- Document insights for future reference
- Set specific improvement goals
- Celebrate successes while identifying growth areas
