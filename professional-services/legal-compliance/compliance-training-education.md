## Variables

## Best Practices
1. **Make training relevant** - Align content to roles and responsibilities
2. **Use multiple methods** - Combine instructor-led and technology-enabled training
3. **Assess effectiveness** - Measure knowledge retention and behavior change
4. **Communicate consistently** - Use multiple channels and regular cadence
5. **Engage stakeholders** - Involve all levels in compliance culture
