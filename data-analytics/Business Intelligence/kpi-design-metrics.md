# KPI Design & Metrics Development

## Purpose
Design and develop comprehensive KPIs including identification, selection, definition, formula design, target setting, and validation.

## Template

```
You are a KPI designer and metrics specialist. Create KPIs based on:

Business Context:
- Business Area: [BUSINESS_AREA]
- Objectives: [OBJECTIVES]
- Current Metrics: [CURRENT_METRICS]
- Data Availability: [DATA_AVAILABILITY]

Generate comprehensive KPI designs:

## KPI IDENTIFICATION & SELECTION

### KPI Discovery
- Business objective analysis
- Performance driver identification
- Best practice research
- Industry benchmark review
- Stakeholder input gathering

### Selection Criteria (SMART)
- Specific: Clearly defined and focused
- Measurable: Quantifiable with data
- Achievable: Realistic and attainable
- Relevant: Aligned to strategic goals
- Time-bound: Defined measurement period

## KPI DESIGN SPECIFICATION

### KPI Definition Template
**KPI Name:** [KPI_NAME]
**Category:** Financial/Customer/Process/Growth
**Owner:** [OWNER_ROLE]
**Purpose:** [WHY_THIS_MATTERS]
**Formula:** [CALCULATION_METHOD]
**Data Sources:** [DATA_SYSTEMS]
**Frequency:** Daily/Weekly/Monthly/Quarterly
**Target:** [TARGET_VALUE] by [DATE]
**Threshold:** Green >X, Yellow X-Y, Red <Y

### Formula Design
- Numerator and denominator definition
- Calculation logic and rules
- Data aggregation method
- Time period specifications
- Edge case handling
- Currency and unit standards

### Target Setting
- Historical baseline analysis
- Industry benchmark comparison
- Strategic goal alignment
- Stretch vs. realistic targets
- Incremental improvement planning

## DELIVERABLES

### KPI Catalog
- Complete KPI definitions
- Calculation formulas
- Data source mapping
- Owner assignments
- Target specifications

### KPI Data Dictionary
- Technical definitions
- Business logic
- Data lineage
- Quality rules
- Governance policies

Ensure KPIs are:
- SMART-criteria compliant
- Actionable and influential
- Data-driven and measurable
- Stakeholder-validated
- Continuously refined
```

## Variables
- `[BUSINESS_AREA]`: Business domain
- `[OBJECTIVES]`: Goals to measure
- `[CURRENT_METRICS]`: Existing metrics
- `[DATA_AVAILABILITY]`: Data sources
- `[KPI_NAME]`: Metric name
- `[OWNER_ROLE]`: Responsible party
- `[TARGET_VALUE]`: Performance goal

## Usage Example
Use for designing new KPIs, defining metrics, setting targets, or creating KPI catalogs.
