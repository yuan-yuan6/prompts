## BEST PRACTICES

### Writing Excellence
- Lead with news value and reader benefit
- Use active voice and strong action verbs
- Include specific, quantifiable details when possible
- Maintain journalistic style and objectivity
- Keep sentences and paragraphs concise
- Use industry-appropriate terminology
- Ensure accuracy and fact-checking
- Provide clear attribution for all claims

### Media Relations
- Build relationships before you need coverage
- Provide value beyond just company news
- Respect journalist deadlines and preferences
- Offer exclusive access and unique angles
- Be transparent about challenges and limitations
- Follow up professionally without being pushy
- Track and measure relationship quality
- Adapt approach based on feedback and results

### Strategic Positioning
- Align all communications with brand values
- Position spokespeople as industry experts
- Connect company news to larger industry trends
- Anticipate and prepare for difficult questions
- Maintain consistent messaging across all channels
- Use data and evidence to support claims
- Plan for both success and crisis scenarios
- Measure impact on business objectives
