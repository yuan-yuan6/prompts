```
POLICY_TYPE: "Comprehensive smoke-free legislation"
HEALTH_ISSUE: "Secondhand smoke exposure"
POLICY_MAKERS: "State legislature and governor"
ADVOCACY_STRATEGY: "Coalition-based grassroots campaign"
POLICY_GOAL: "100% smoke-free workplaces and public places"
```

### Example 2: Healthcare Access
```
POLICY_TYPE: "Medicaid expansion"
HEALTH_ISSUE: "Healthcare coverage gap"
TARGET_DECISION_MAKERS: "State legislators, governor"
POLITICAL_CONTEXT: "Conservative-leaning state"
ADVOCACY_STRATEGY: "Economic argument with business support"
```

### Example 3: Public Health Funding
```
POLICY_TYPE: "Public health infrastructure investment"
HEALTH_ISSUE: "Inadequate public health capacity"
POLICY_LEVEL: "Federal appropriations"
ADVOCACY_STRATEGY: "Multi-state coalition with data-driven approach"
POLICY_GOAL: "Double CDC funding over 5 years"
```
