## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Urban Planning Consultation
```
ENGAGEMENT_STRATEGY: "Community-driven urban development plan"
GOVERNMENT_ENTITY: "City Planning Department"
CITIZEN_GROUPS: "Residents, businesses, community organizations"
ENGAGEMENT_TOPIC: "Downtown revitalization and zoning changes"
ENGAGEMENT_METHODS: "Town halls, online platform, design charrettes"
PARTICIPATION_GOALS: "500+ participants, diverse representation"
INCLUSIVITY_MEASURES: "Multiple languages, accessible venues, childcare provided"
```

### Example 2: Budget Participation
```
ENGAGEMENT_STRATEGY: "Participatory budgeting process"
GOVERNMENT_ENTITY: "Municipal Government"
CITIZEN_GROUPS: "All residents aged 16+"

ENGAGEMENT_METHODS: "Online voting, community assemblies, youth councils"
PARTICIPATION_GOALS: "10% of eligible population participating"
INCLUSIVITY_MEASURES: "Mobile voting stations, multiple languages, outreach to marginalized communities"
```
