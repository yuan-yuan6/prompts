# Compliance Governance & Organization Design

## Purpose
Design comprehensive compliance governance structures covering board oversight, organizational models, policy management, and culture development to ensure effective compliance program leadership and accountability.

## Template

```
You are a compliance governance specialist. Create a detailed compliance governance and organization framework based on:

Organization Context:
- Organization: [ORGANIZATION_NAME]
- Industry: [INDUSTRY_CLASSIFICATION]
- Size: [ENTITY_SCALE]
- Risk Profile: [COMPLIANCE_RISK_LEVEL]

Generate comprehensive guidance covering:

## 1. COMPLIANCE GOVERNANCE AND ORGANIZATION

### 1.1 Governance Structure Design

#### Board and Executive Oversight
##### Board Responsibilities and Accountability
- Compliance oversight and strategic direction
- Risk appetite and tolerance setting
- Resource allocation and investment approval
- Performance monitoring and evaluation
- Regulatory relationship and stakeholder management
- Crisis management and incident response oversight
- Compliance culture promotion and reinforcement
- Executive compensation and incentive alignment

##### Executive Leadership Engagement
- Chief Compliance Officer role and authority
- Executive committee compliance responsibilities
- Business unit leader compliance accountability
- Functional area compliance coordination
- Cross-functional collaboration and integration
- Resource commitment and support provision
- Performance expectation setting and monitoring
- Compliance culture modeling and demonstration

#### Organizational Structure Framework
##### Compliance Function Organization
- Centralized vs. decentralized compliance model
- Compliance officer roles and responsibilities
- Business unit compliance coordinator network
- Subject matter expert specialization
- Geographic and jurisdictional coverage
- Functional area integration and support
- Career development and succession planning
- Resource optimization and efficiency maximization

##### Three Lines of Defense Model
###### First Line of Defense - Business Operations
- Business unit compliance responsibility and ownership
- Process control and risk management integration
- Policy adherence and procedure implementation
- Self-assessment and monitoring execution
- Issue identification and escalation procedures
- Corrective action implementation and follow-up
- Compliance training and awareness participation
- Performance measurement and reporting contribution

###### Second Line of Defense - Compliance Function
- Compliance program design and oversight
- Policy development and maintenance
- Risk assessment and monitoring coordination
- Training program design and delivery
- Advisory and consultation service provision
- Regulatory interpretation and guidance
- Issue investigation and resolution support
- Performance measurement and reporting

###### Third Line of Defense - Internal Audit
- Independent compliance program evaluation
- Control effectiveness testing and validation
- Risk assessment verification and challenge
- Regulatory examination preparation and support
- Best practice identification and recommendation
- Compliance program improvement and optimization
- Board and executive reporting and communication
- External audit and regulatory coordination

### 1.2 Policy and Procedure Framework

#### Policy Management System
##### Policy Development Lifecycle
- Regulatory requirement identification and analysis
- Policy scope and applicability definition
- Stakeholder consultation and input collection
- Policy drafting and content development
- Legal and compliance review and approval
- Business impact assessment and validation
- Implementation planning and resource allocation
- Communication and training requirement identification

##### Policy Content Standards
- Policy purpose and objective articulation
- Regulatory requirement mapping and reference
- Scope and applicability definition
- Roles and responsibility assignment
- Process and procedure specification
- Control and monitoring requirement
- Exception and variance management
- Review and update frequency determination

#### Procedure Implementation Framework
##### Standard Operating Procedures
- Process workflow design and documentation
- Step-by-step instruction and guidance
- Role-based task assignment and accountability
- Control point identification and verification
- Quality assurance and error prevention
- Documentation and record-keeping requirements
- Training and competency requirements
- Performance measurement and improvement

##### Process Control Integration
- Business process risk and control mapping
- Compliance checkpoint integration and automation
- Exception handling and escalation procedures
- Quality assurance and validation protocols
- Performance monitoring and measurement
- Continuous improvement and optimization
- Technology enablement and efficiency enhancement
- User experience and adoption optimization

### 1.3 Compliance Culture Development

#### Culture Assessment and Design
##### Current Culture Evaluation
- Compliance culture maturity assessment
- Employee attitude and behavior survey
- Leadership commitment and modeling evaluation
- Communication effectiveness and reach analysis
- Training impact and knowledge retention assessment
- Incentive alignment and consequence evaluation
- Decision-making process and criteria review
- Performance measurement and recognition analysis

##### Target Culture Definition
- Compliance culture vision and values articulation
- Behavioral expectation definition and communication
- Decision-making criteria and framework establishment
- Communication style and frequency specification
- Training approach and methodology selection
- Incentive structure and consequence alignment
- Recognition and reward program design
- Performance measurement and feedback system

#### Culture Change Management
##### Change Strategy Development
- Change readiness assessment and gap analysis
- Change impact evaluation and stakeholder mapping
- Communication strategy and messaging development
- Training and skill development program design
- Leadership engagement and role modeling
- Incentive alignment and behavior reinforcement
- Progress monitoring and course correction
- Success measurement and celebration

##### Behavioral Reinforcement Mechanisms
- Performance evaluation integration and weighting
- Incentive and compensation alignment
- Recognition and reward program implementation
- Coaching and mentoring support provision
- Feedback and communication enhancement
- Training and development opportunity creation
- Career advancement and succession consideration
- Disciplinary action and consequence application
```

## Variables
- `[ORGANIZATION_NAME]`: Organization name
- `[INDUSTRY_CLASSIFICATION]`: Industry sector
- `[ENTITY_SCALE]`: Organization size
- `[COMPLIANCE_RISK_LEVEL]`: Risk profile

## Best Practices
1. **Establish clear governance** - Define roles, responsibilities, and reporting lines
2. **Build strong culture** - Integrate compliance into organizational values
3. **Ensure independence** - Maintain compliance function authority and objectivity
4. **Engage leadership** - Secure board and executive commitment
5. **Develop talent** - Invest in compliance professional growth
