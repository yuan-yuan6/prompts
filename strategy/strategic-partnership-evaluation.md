# Strategic Partnership Evaluation Framework

## Purpose
Systematically evaluate, structure, and manage strategic partnerships to create mutual value, access complementary capabilities, and achieve objectives faster or more cost-effectively than going alone, while managing partnership risks and complexities.

## Quick Start

**Want to evaluate a partnership opportunity quickly?** Here's the minimal approach:

### When to Use This
- Considering partnership vs. build vs. buy decisions
- Evaluating specific partnership proposals
- Designing partnership strategy for market expansion
- Optimizing existing underperforming partnerships
- Building ecosystem or platform strategy

### Minimal Example
```
Company: B2B SaaS startup
Opportunity: Partnership with large enterprise software vendor

Quick Assessment:
Strategic Fit: HIGH (access to their enterprise customer base)
Value Exchange: BALANCED (we provide specialized capability, they provide distribution)
Risk: MEDIUM (dependency on their roadmap, competition risk)
Governance: Need clear IP ownership and customer relationship terms

Key Questions:
✅ Do we get access to their customers? Yes, through their marketplace
✅ Can we maintain customer relationships? Yes, with limitations
✅ What happens if they build competing feature? Need protective clauses
⚠️ Are we replaceable? Could build our capability, need to provide ongoing value

Decision: PROCEED with partnership, but negotiate:
1. Non-compete for 3 years
2. Customer data access rights
3. Exclusivity in our category
4. Clear exit terms

Result: Partnership launched, 40% of revenue in Year 1 from their referrals
```

### Basic 5-Step Workflow
1. **Assess Strategic Fit** - Does partnership advance our strategy?
2. **Evaluate Partner Fit** - Is this the right partner?
3. **Design Value Exchange** - What does each side contribute and gain?
4. **Structure Agreement** - Governance, economics, exit terms
5. **Manage & Optimize** - Execute partnership for mutual value

**Time to complete**: 1-2 weeks for initial evaluation, 4-8 weeks to structure partnership

**Pro tip**: Best partnerships have balanced value exchange - if one side gets much more, it won't last

## Best Practices

### Do's ✅
- **Balanced Value Exchange**: Ensure mutual benefit
- **Clear Governance**: Define decision rights upfront
- **Protect IP**: Clear ownership and usage rights
- **Plan for Exit**: Include exit terms from day 1
- **Invest in Relationship**: Partnerships need active management
- **Measure Performance**: Track value delivery
- **Communicate Constantly**: Over-communicate with partner

### Don'ts ❌
- **Don't Rush**: Take time to structure properly
- **Don't Assume Alignment**: Verify strategic fit
- **Don't Ignore Culture**: Cultural mismatch kills partnerships
- **Don't Create Dependency**: Maintain alternatives
- **Don't Neglect Legal**: Proper agreements prevent conflicts
- **Don't Set and Forget**: Partnerships need ongoing management
