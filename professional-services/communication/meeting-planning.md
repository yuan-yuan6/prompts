# Meeting Planning & Preparation

## Purpose
Design comprehensive meeting planning frameworks covering objective setting, agenda development, participant selection, and meeting preparation.

## Template

```
You are a meeting effectiveness specialist. Create detailed meeting planning guidance based on:

Meeting Context:
- Meeting Type: [MEETING_PURPOSE_AND_FORMAT]
- Participants: [ATTENDEE_ROLES_AND_COUNT]
- Duration: [MEETING_LENGTH]
- Frequency: [ONE_TIME_OR_RECURRING]

Generate comprehensive planning guidance covering:

## 1. MEETING PLANNING AND PREPARATION

### 1.1 Meeting Purpose and Objectives
- Clear purpose statement and rationale
- Specific, measurable objectives
- Desired outcomes and deliverables
- Decision-making requirements
- Information sharing needs
- Problem-solving focus
- Relationship building goals
- Success criteria definition

### 1.2 Agenda Development
- Agenda structure and format
- Time allocation by topic
- Topic prioritization and sequencing
- Discussion vs. decision items
- Presenter and owner assignment
- Pre-reading requirements
- Interactive elements planning
- Buffer time inclusion

### 1.3 Participant Selection
- Role-based attendance criteria
- Decision-maker identification
- Subject matter expert inclusion
- Stakeholder representation
- Optional vs. required attendance
- Right-sizing meeting attendance
- Alternative participation methods
- Communication to non-attendees

### 1.4 Meeting Preparation
- Pre-work and reading materials
- Background information sharing
- Data and analysis preparation
- Decision options development
- Presentation deck creation
- Technology and tool setup
- Logistics and facility arrangements
- Participant reminder communications
```

## Variables
- `[MEETING_PURPOSE_AND_FORMAT]`: Meeting type
- `[ATTENDEE_ROLES_AND_COUNT]`: Participants
- `[MEETING_LENGTH]`: Duration
- `[ONE_TIME_OR_RECURRING]`: Frequency

## Best Practices
1. **Define clear purpose** - Every meeting needs a reason
2. **Create focused agenda** - Structure time effectively
3. **Invite the right people** - Quality over quantity
4. **Prepare thoroughly** - Share pre-work in advance
5. **Set expectations** - Communicate objectives clearly
