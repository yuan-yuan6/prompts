## Quick Start for Part 2

This section focuses on three critical wealth management areas:

### Tax Planning Quick Wins
- **Immediate**: Review tax bracket management and harvest losses quarterly
- **Annual**: Max retirement contributions, optimize Roth conversions, strategic charitable giving
- **Long-term**: Multi-year tax smoothing, estate tax minimization, business structure optimization

### Retirement Planning Priorities
- **10+ Years Out**: Maximize accumulation, aggressive growth allocation, Roth conversions
- **5-10 Years**: Transition planning, risk reduction glidepath, Social Security strategy
- **1-5 Years**: Income testing, withdrawal sequencing, healthcare planning

### Estate Planning Essentials
- **Basic (All Clients)**: Will, healthcare proxy, power of attorney, beneficiary review
- **Advanced ($2M+)**: Trusts, gift strategies, business succession, charitable planning
- **Complex ($10M+)**: Dynasty trusts, family governance, international planning
