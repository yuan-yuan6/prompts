# Digital Transformation Roadmap & Execution

## Purpose
Design comprehensive digital transformation roadmaps covering initiative planning, technology architecture, agile delivery, and program governance.

## Template

```
You are a digital transformation delivery specialist. Create detailed roadmap based on:

Roadmap Context:
- Priority Initiatives: [KEY_TRANSFORMATION_PROGRAMS]
- Technology Stack: [CURRENT_AND_TARGET_PLATFORMS]
- Delivery Approach: [AGILE_WATERFALL_HYBRID]
- Timeline: [TRANSFORMATION_HORIZON]

Generate comprehensive roadmap guidance covering:

## 3. ROADMAP AND EXECUTION

### 3.1 Transformation Roadmap Development
- Initiative identification and definition
- Phasing and wave planning
- Dependency management and sequencing
- Resource allocation and capacity planning
- Budget development and investment timing
- Quick wins and momentum building
- Risk mitigation and contingency planning
- Milestone definition and tracking

### 3.2 Technology Architecture Evolution
- Current architecture assessment
- Target architecture vision
- Cloud migration and modernization
- Legacy system rationalization
- Integration and API strategy
- Data platform and analytics
- Security and compliance architecture
- Scalability and performance planning

### 3.3 Agile Delivery Framework
- Product and portfolio management
- Squad and tribe organization
- Sprint planning and execution
- Backlog management and prioritization
- User story development and refinement
- Testing and quality assurance
- Continuous integration and deployment
- Retrospectives and improvement

### 3.4 Program Governance and Management
- Steering committee and oversight
- Program management office structure
- Portfolio and initiative tracking
- Resource management and allocation
- Budget monitoring and control
- Risk and issue management
- Decision-making framework
- Reporting and communication
```

## Variables
- `[KEY_TRANSFORMATION_PROGRAMS]`: Priority initiatives
- `[CURRENT_AND_TARGET_PLATFORMS]`: Technology stack
- `[AGILE_WATERFALL_HYBRID]`: Delivery approach
- `[TRANSFORMATION_HORIZON]`: Timeline

## Best Practices
1. **Phase strategically** - Build momentum with quick wins
2. **Manage dependencies** - Sequence initiatives logically
3. **Deliver iteratively** - Use agile approaches
4. **Govern effectively** - Maintain oversight and alignment
5. **Adapt dynamically** - Adjust based on learnings
