# Audit & Compliance Overview

## Purpose
This overview provides guidance on the complete audit and compliance framework for financial management and organizational governance.

## Framework Components

### 1. Audit Planning & Risk (audit-planning-risk.md)
- Risk-based audit planning
- Resource allocation
- Scope definition

### 2. Internal Controls Evaluation (internal-controls-evaluation.md)
- Control design assessment
- Operating effectiveness testing
- Deficiency identification

### 3. Audit Testing & Documentation (audit-testing-documentation.md)
- Testing procedures
- Evidence collection
- Work paper management

### 4. External Audit Coordination (external-audit-coordination.md)
- Auditor coordination
- Request management
- Findings resolution

### 5. Compliance Monitoring Program (compliance-monitoring-program.md)
- Continuous monitoring
- Performance tracking
- Improvement initiatives

## Best Practices
1. **Risk-based approach** - Focus on highest-risk areas
2. **Comprehensive testing** - Ensure thorough coverage
3. **Clear documentation** - Maintain detailed records
4. **Effective communication** - Keep stakeholders informed
5. **Continuous improvement** - Learn and adapt
