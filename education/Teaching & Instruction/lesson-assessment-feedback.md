# Lesson Assessment & Feedback

## Purpose
Design valid, reliable assessments that measure learning outcomes and provide timely, constructive feedback to support student growth.

## Best Practices
- Assess frequently and formatively
- Align assessments with objectives
- Use varied assessment methods
- Provide timely, specific feedback
- Involve students in self-assessment
- Use data to inform instruction
- Celebrate growth and progress
- Focus on learning, not just grades
