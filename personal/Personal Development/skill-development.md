Target skill: Jazz piano performance

## Customization Options

- Adapt for different skill domains (cognitive, physical, creative, social)
- Scale complexity based on learning experience
- Include age-specific considerations
- Adjust for learning disabilities or challenges
- Incorporate cultural learning preferences
- Add domain-specific practice methods
- Customize for individual vs. group learning
- Include certification or competition preparation
