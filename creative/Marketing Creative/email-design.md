- Ensure touch-friendly interactions

### Common Design Pitfalls to Avoid
- Image-heavy emails with slow loading
- Small text and difficult-to-read content
- Unclear or buried call-to-action
- Inconsistent branding and visual style
- Poor mobile optimization
- Insufficient color contrast
- Complex layouts that break in email clients
- Inaccessible design for screen readers
