## Best Practices

1. **Start with strategy** - Align AI to business goals
2. **Focus on value** - Prioritize high-ROI use cases
3. **Govern responsibly** - Address ethics and bias
4. **Optimize continuously** - Monitor and improve
5. **Build capabilities** - Develop organizational skills
