# Risk Identification & Analysis

## Purpose
Identify and analyze comprehensive business risks including market risk, credit risk, operational risk, strategic risk, and compliance risk for effective risk management.

## Template

```
You are a risk management professional. Conduct a comprehensive risk identification and analysis for:

Assessment Context:
- Organization: [ORGANIZATION_NAME]
- Scope: [ASSESSMENT_SCOPE]
- Risk Categories: [RISK_CATEGORIES]
- Time Frame: [TIMEFRAME]

Generate a comprehensive risk identification and analysis:

## RISK IDENTIFICATION

### Market Risks
- Price volatility and market fluctuations
- Interest rate changes
- Foreign exchange exposure
- Commodity price movements
- Liquidity constraints
- Market concentration
- Systemic market events

### Credit Risks
- Counterparty default risk
- Credit rating downgrades
- Concentration risk
- Settlement risk
- Sovereign risk
- Correlation risk

### Operational Risks
- Process failures
- System outages
- Human error
- Fraud and misconduct
- Cybersecurity threats
- Third-party vendor risks
- Business continuity threats

### Compliance & Regulatory Risks
- Regulatory changes
- Non-compliance penalties
- Legal liabilities
- Reporting failures
- Policy violations

### Strategic Risks
- Business model disruption
- Competitive threats
- Technology obsolescence
- Reputation damage
- M&A integration risks

## RISK ANALYSIS

### Qualitative Analysis
- Risk description and characteristics
- Cause and effect relationships
- Impact scenarios
- Vulnerability assessment
- Control effectiveness evaluation

### Quantitative Analysis
- Probability estimation
- Impact magnitude calculation
- Expected loss calculation
- Value at Risk (VaR)
- Stress testing scenarios
- Sensitivity analysis

## DELIVERABLES

- Risk register with identified risks
- Risk analysis reports
- Heat maps and matrices
- Scenario analyses
- Control assessments

Ensure analysis is comprehensive, data-driven, and actionable.
```

## Usage Example
Use for risk identification, analysis, or assessment programs.
