# Grant Writing & Proposal Development Framework

## Purpose
Comprehensive system for researching, writing, and managing grant proposals for nonprofit organizations, including federal, state, foundation, and corporate grant applications.

## Template

Develop grant proposal for [ORGANIZATION_NAME] seeking $[REQUEST_AMOUNT] from [FUNDER_NAME] for [PROJECT_NAME] serving [TARGET_POPULATION] in [GEOGRAPHIC_AREA] over [PROJECT_DURATION].

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Federal Government Grant
```
Organization: National Health Initiative
Funder: CDC
Amount: $2.5 million
Duration: 5 years
Focus: Community health intervention
Requirements: RCT evaluation, quarterly reports
```

### Example 2: Foundation Grant
```
Organization: Youth Education Network
Funder: Gates Foundation
Amount: $500,000
Duration: 3 years
Focus: STEM education innovation
Requirements: Theory of change, impact metrics
```

### Example 3: Corporate Grant
```
Organization: Environmental Alliance
Funder: Corporate CSR Program
Amount: $100,000
Duration: 1 year
Focus: Local sustainability project
Requirements: Brand alignment, volunteer engagement
```
