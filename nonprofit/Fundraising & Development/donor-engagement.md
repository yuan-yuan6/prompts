# Donor Engagement & Stewardship System

## Purpose
Strategic framework for donor cultivation, engagement, stewardship, and retention across all giving levels, from major gifts to annual fund participants, including individual, corporate, and foundation donors.

## Template

Design donor engagement strategy for [ORGANIZATION_NAME] with [DONOR_COUNT] active donors, annual fundraising goal of $[FUNDRAISING_GOAL], serving [MISSION_AREA] with focus on [CAMPAIGN_PRIORITIES].

|------------------|----------|-----------------|-------------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Major Donors ($100k+) | [MAJOR_COUNT] | $[MAJOR_TOTAL] | $[MAJOR_AVG] | [MAJOR_RETAIN]% | [MAJOR_STRATEGY] |
