# Telemedicine Patient Experience & Engagement

## Purpose
Design comprehensive patient experience frameworks covering onboarding, education, engagement, satisfaction measurement, and quality improvement.

## Template

```
You are a patient experience specialist. Create detailed patient engagement framework based on:

Patient Context:
- Patient Demographics: [TARGET_PATIENT_POPULATION]
- Technology Literacy: [DIGITAL_PROFICIENCY_LEVELS]
- Access Barriers: [PATIENT_CHALLENGES_TO_ADDRESS]

Generate comprehensive patient experience guidance covering:

## 5. PATIENT EXPERIENCE AND ENGAGEMENT

### 5.1 Patient Onboarding and Education
- Technology requirements and setup
- Portal registration and navigation
- Video visit preparation checklist
- Privacy and security education
- Technical support resources
- Language and accessibility options
- Caregiver involvement guidance
- Frequently asked questions

### 5.2 Patient Engagement Strategies
- Pre-visit questionnaires and intake
- Educational material delivery
- Shared decision-making tools
- Care plan review and agreement
- Medication adherence support
- Lifestyle modification programs
- Self-monitoring and reporting
- Patient community building

### 5.3 Digital Literacy Support
- Technology training programs
- Technical troubleshooting guides
- Device loaner programs
- In-person setup assistance
- Family and caregiver training
- Simplified user interfaces
- Multiple access channels
- Ongoing technical support

### 5.4 Patient Satisfaction and Feedback
- Post-visit satisfaction surveys
- Real-time feedback mechanisms
- Complaint management processes
- Patient advisory councils
- Focus groups and interviews
- Net Promoter Score tracking
- Quality improvement integration
- Recognition and celebration

### 5.5 Quality Measurement and Improvement
- Clinical quality metrics
- Patient experience scores
- Access and availability measures
- Technical performance indicators
- Provider satisfaction metrics
- Cost effectiveness analysis
- Outcome measurement and tracking
- Continuous improvement processes
```

## Variables
- `[TARGET_PATIENT_POPULATION]`: Patient demographics
- `[DIGITAL_PROFICIENCY_LEVELS]`: Technology literacy
- `[PATIENT_CHALLENGES_TO_ADDRESS]`: Access barriers

## Best Practices
1. **Simplify onboarding** - Make technology easy to use
2. **Provide support** - Offer technical assistance
3. **Engage patients actively** - Use multiple touchpoints
4. **Measure satisfaction** - Collect and act on feedback
5. **Continuously improve** - Track quality metrics and iterate
