## Best Practices

1. **Start with strategy** - Align design with business objectives
2. **Research thoroughly** - Understand audience, market, and competition
3. **Define scope clearly** - Document deliverables and responsibilities
4. **Plan realistic timelines** - Account for reviews and revisions
5. **Budget comprehensively** - Include all costs and contingency
6. **Communicate regularly** - Keep stakeholders informed and aligned
7. **Quality over speed** - Prioritize excellence and thoroughness
8. **Document decisions** - Maintain clear rationale and approvals
9. **Test and validate** - Verify designs work across applications
10. **Measure results** - Track performance against objectives

## Tips for Success

- Conduct thorough discovery before designing
- Present concepts with strategic rationale
- Build in adequate revision cycles
- Create comprehensive style guides
- Test designs in real-world contexts
- Plan for phased implementation
- Train teams on proper usage
- Monitor consistency after launch
- Gather feedback and iterate
- Celebrate milestones and wins
