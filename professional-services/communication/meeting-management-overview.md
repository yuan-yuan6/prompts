# Meeting Management Framework - Overview

## Purpose
Create comprehensive meeting management frameworks covering planning, facilitation, documentation, and continuous improvement to maximize meeting effectiveness and value.

## Framework Components

This framework has been split into focused modules:

### 1. Planning & Preparation
**File:** `meeting-planning.md`
- Meeting purpose and objectives
- Agenda development
- Participant selection
- Meeting preparation

### 2. Facilitation & Execution
**File:** `meeting-facilitation.md`
- Meeting opening
- Time management
- Participation management
- Decision-making

### 3. Documentation & Follow-Up
**File:** `meeting-documentation.md`
- Meeting notes and minutes
- Action item tracking
- Follow-up communication
- Knowledge management

### 4. Effectiveness & Improvement
**File:** `meeting-effectiveness.md`
- Effectiveness assessment
- Feedback and improvement
- Meeting optimization
- Governance and culture

## Meeting Types

**Decision Meetings** - Make important choices
**Planning Meetings** - Develop strategies and plans
**Problem-Solving Meetings** - Address challenges
**Information Sharing** - Update and align
**Brainstorming Sessions** - Generate ideas
**Team Building** - Strengthen relationships
**Status Updates** - Review progress
**Retrospectives** - Learn and improve

## Best Practices

1. **Question necessity** - Does this need to be a meeting?
2. **Prepare thoroughly** - Plan agenda and pre-work
3. **Facilitate actively** - Manage time and participation
4. **Document clearly** - Capture decisions and actions
5. **Follow up consistently** - Track completion and impact
6. **Measure effectiveness** - Continuously improve
7. **Respect time** - Start and end on schedule
8. **Engage everyone** - Create inclusive participation
9. **Make decisions** - Don't leave without outcomes
10. **Improve continuously** - Learn from every meeting
