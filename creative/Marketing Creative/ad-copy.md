## Usage Examples

### Example 1: SaaS Product Launch
```
AD_TYPE: "Digital advertising campaign"
BRAND_NAME: "ProductivityMax"
TARGET_AUDIENCE: "Small business owners and entrepreneurs"
CAMPAIGN_OBJECTIVE: "Drive free trial signups"
BRAND_VOICE: "Professional yet approachable"
KEY_MESSAGE: "Streamline your workflow and boost productivity"
```

### Example 2: E-commerce Fashion Brand
```

CAMPAIGN_OBJECTIVE: "Increase online sales and brand awareness"
BRAND_VOICE: "Trendy and aspirational"

```

### Example 3: Local Service Business
