# Product Management Templates

Comprehensive templates for product managers covering strategy, development, analytics, and operations across the product lifecycle.

## Overview

This category provides templates for product managers at all levels, from associate product managers building their first roadmap to CPOs defining product strategy. Templates cover the complete product lifecycle from ideation through sunset.

## Subcategories

### Product Strategy
Strategic planning, vision setting, and market positioning for product portfolios and individual products.

**Templates:**
- Product strategy and vision
- Product roadmapping
- Market and competitive analysis
- Product-market fit assessment

### Product Development
Execution-focused templates for building and launching products.

**Templates:**
- Feature prioritization frameworks
- User research and personas
- Product requirements (PRD)
- Go-to-market planning
- Product launch execution

### Product Analytics
Data-driven decision making, metrics, and experimentation.

**Templates:**
- Product metrics and KPIs
- A/B testing and experimentation
- Product analytics frameworks
- User behavior analysis

### Product Operations
Day-to-day product management operations and team coordination.

**Templates:**
- Agile/Scrum for product teams
- Stakeholder management
- Product documentation
- Product lifecycle management

## Who Should Use These Templates

- Product Managers (APM, PM, SPM, GPM)
- Product Leaders (Director, VP, CPO)
- Product Marketing Managers
- Product Designers
- Engineering Managers working with product teams
- Startup Founders in product roles
