# AI/ML Applications Templates

Comprehensive templates for building, deploying, and scaling AI/ML applications including LLM applications, AI product development, MLOps, and AI strategy.

## Overview

This category focuses on **building AI products and applications**, distinct from data science and analytics. Templates cover the full AI application lifecycle from strategy through production deployment, with emphasis on LLMs, generative AI, and modern AI engineering practices.

## Subcategories

Building applications with large language models including prompt engineering, RAG systems, agents, and conversational AI.

**Templates:**
- LLM application development
- Prompt engineering workflows
- RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) systems
- AI agents and autonomous systems
- Conversational AI and chatbots

### AI Product Development
Product-specific templates for building AI-powered products and features.

**Templates:**

- AI feature development

- Responsible AI and ethics

### MLOps & Deployment
Operational templates for deploying, monitoring, and maintaining AI systems in production.

**Templates:**
- MLOps and model deployment

- Model versioning and management
- AI performance optimization
- AI cost optimization

### AI Strategy
Strategic planning for AI initiatives and transformation.

**Templates:**
- AI strategy and roadmap
- AI use case identification
- AI capability building
- AI vendor and tool selection
- AI ROI and business case
