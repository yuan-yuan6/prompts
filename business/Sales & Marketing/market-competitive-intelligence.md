Gather, analyze, and act on competitive intelligence to inform strategic positioning, identify opportunities, and maintain competitive advantage.

## Intelligence Best Practices
- Gather intelligence ethically and legally
- Use multiple sources for validation
- Update competitor profiles regularly
- Monitor competitive moves continuously
- Analyze competitor financial health
- Track product launches and changes
- Benchmark against best-in-class
- Develop early warning systems
