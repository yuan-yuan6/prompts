# Corporate Partnerships & Sponsorships

## Purpose
Framework for building corporate partnerships, securing sponsorships, creating cause marketing collaborations, employee giving programs, and mutually beneficial nonprofit-corporate relationships.

## Quick Start

### Minimal Example
```
Develop corporate partnership program targeting 20 companies for environmental nonprofit. Include tiered sponsorship packages, employee volunteer programs, cause marketing campaigns, matching gift promotion, and corporate foundation grants generating $500K annually.
```

### Basic 3-Step Workflow
1. **Partnership Strategy** - Target companies, value proposition (3-4 weeks)
2. **Outreach & Cultivation** - Prospecting, proposals, negotiation (2-6 months)
3. **Partnership Management** - Activation, stewardship, renewal (12+ months)

---

## Key Components

- Corporate prospect research and targeting
- Partnership value proposition
- Sponsorship package development
- Cause marketing opportunities
- Employee engagement programs
- Proposal and pitch deck
- Partnership activation and fulfillment
- ROI measurement and reporting
