# Fundraising Campaign & Donor Management Framework

## Purpose
Comprehensive framework for planning and executing nonprofit fundraising campaigns including donor cultivation, campaign strategy, multi-channel outreach, stewardship programs, and impact measurement for sustainable philanthropic support.

## Template

Launch fundraising campaign [CAMPAIGN_NAME] targeting $[FUNDRAISING_GOAL] from [DONOR_COUNT] donors over [CAMPAIGN_DURATION] timeline, achieving [PARTICIPATION_RATE]% participation, [RETENTION_RATE]% donor retention, [AVERAGE_GIFT] average gift size, [ROI_TARGET] ROI, supporting [BENEFICIARY_COUNT] beneficiaries with [IMPACT_METRICS] measurable impact.

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Capital Campaign
```
Campaign: $50M facility expansion
Timeline: 3-year campaign
Strategy: Quiet phase, public phase, celebration
Lead Gifts: 60% from top 10 donors
Participation: 5,000 total donors
Success: $52M raised, 104% of goal
Impact: New facility serving 10,000 annually
Legacy: 25 named spaces, 3 endowments
```

### Example 2: Annual Giving Campaign
```
Goal: $2M unrestricted funds
Donors: 10,000 active donors
Channels: Direct mail, email, phone, online
Average Gift: $200
Retention: 65% donor retention rate
Growth: 15% year-over-year increase
Cost: $0.25 per dollar raised
Innovation: Monthly giving program launch
```

### Example 3: Disaster Relief Campaign
```
Emergency: Natural disaster response
Target: $5M in 30 days
Platform: Online crowdfunding focus
Response: 50,000 individual donors
Corporate: $1M in matching gifts
Distribution: 100% to affected communities
Transparency: Real-time impact dashboard
Follow-up: Long-term recovery program
```
