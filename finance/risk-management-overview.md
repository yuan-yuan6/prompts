# Risk Management Framework - Overview

## Purpose
Create comprehensive risk management strategies covering credit risk, market risk, operational risk, regulatory compliance, stress testing, and Basel framework implementation to ensure financial stability and regulatory adherence.

## Framework Components

This framework has been split into focused modules:

### 1. Risk Identification & ERM Foundation
**File:** `risk-identification.md`
- Governance and organization
- Risk appetite and tolerance
- Risk culture development
- Education and training

### 2. Risk Assessment & Credit Risk
**File:** `risk-assessment-quantification.md`
- Credit risk framework and policies
- Risk measurement and modeling
- Portfolio management
- Market risk quantification

### 3. Risk Mitigation & Operational Risk
**File:** `risk-mitigation-controls.md`
- Operational risk framework
- Process and control management
- Fraud prevention
- Technology and cybersecurity
- Business continuity

### 4. Risk Monitoring & Model Risk
**File:** `risk-monitoring.md`
- Asset liability management
- Liquidity risk management
- Model risk governance
- Advanced analytics
- Emerging risks

### 5. Risk Reporting & Compliance
**File:** `risk-reporting-governance.md`
- Basel framework implementation
- Stress testing and CCAR
- Regulatory reporting
- Examination management
- Risk culture and leadership

## Best Practices

1. **Establish strong governance** - Clear roles and accountability
2. **Define risk appetite** - Set quantitative limits
3. **Measure rigorously** - Use validated models
4. **Monitor continuously** - Real-time dashboards
5. **Comply proactively** - Stay ahead of regulations
6. **Build culture** - Embed risk awareness
7. **Control effectively** - Strong preventive controls
8. **Report transparently** - Clear communication
9. **Innovate responsibly** - Adopt new technologies safely
10. **Improve continuously** - Learn and evolve
