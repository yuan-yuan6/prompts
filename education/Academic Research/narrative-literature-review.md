- `[RESEARCH_TOPIC]`: Specific research topic and focus area

- `[EPISTEMOLOGICAL_APPROACH]`: Research paradigm and philosophical orientation

- `[REGIONAL_FOCUS]`: Geographic scope and regional emphasis
