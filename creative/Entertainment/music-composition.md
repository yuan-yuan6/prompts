# Music Composition & Songwriting Template

## Purpose
Create comprehensive music composition and songwriting solutions covering melody, harmony, lyrics, and arrangement development across all musical genres.

## Variables
- `[PROJECT_TITLE]`: Title of the musical project
- `[GENRE_STYLE]`: Musical genre and style
- `[TARGET_DURATION]`: Target length of composition
- `[KEY_TONALITY]`: Musical key and tonality
- `[TEMPO_BPM]`: Tempo in beats per minute
- `[TIME_SIGNATURE]`: Time signature
- `[MOOD_EMOTION]`: Desired mood and emotion
- `[LYRICAL_THEME]`: Theme for lyrics

## Best Practices
1. **Start with a strong concept** - Have a clear artistic vision
2. **Develop memorable melodies** - Create catchy, singable melodic lines
3. **Use harmonic variety** - Employ interesting chord progressions
4. **Balance repetition and variety** - Make it memorable but not monotonous
5. **Consider the listener** - Keep audience engagement in mind
6. **Refine through iteration** - Polish and improve your compositions
7. **Study great works** - Learn from successful compositions in your genre
8. **Experiment with innovation** - Don't be afraid to try new approaches
