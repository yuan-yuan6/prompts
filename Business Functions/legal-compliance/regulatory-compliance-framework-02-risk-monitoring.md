# Regulatory Compliance Framework - Part 2: Risk Management & Monitoring

## Purpose
Develop comprehensive risk assessment and monitoring systems, implement regulatory change management processes, create training and awareness programs, and establish continuous compliance surveillance capabilities.

## Quick Start

Get started with risk management and monitoring in three simple steps:

1. **Assess Compliance Risks** - Identify, evaluate, and prioritize regulatory risks using quantitative and qualitative methods
2. **Build Monitoring Systems** - Implement real-time monitoring, key risk indicators, and surveillance systems
3. **Design Training Programs** - Create role-based training, awareness campaigns, and competency assessments

**Time to complete:** 6-8 weeks for operational systems
**Output:** Risk register, monitoring dashboards, training curriculum

## Best Practices

1. **Start with high-impact risks** - Prioritize based on probability and severity
2. **Automate monitoring** - Use technology for real-time surveillance
3. **Make training engaging** - Use scenarios, simulations, and interactive content
4. **Establish clear KRIs** - Define measurable early warning indicators
5. **Create feedback loops** - Continuously improve based on monitoring data
6. **Integrate with business** - Embed controls in operational processes
7. **Document regulatory changes** - Maintain comprehensive change log
8. **Measure training effectiveness** - Track behavior change, not just completion
