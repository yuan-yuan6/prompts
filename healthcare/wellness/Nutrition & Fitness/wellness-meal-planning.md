## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Weight Loss Meal Plan
**Context**: 45-year-old woman seeking to lose 30 pounds over 6 months

**Implementation**:
- Assessment: BMR 1,450 calories, sedentary lifestyle, prediabetic, high stress
- Macros: 1,400 calories daily, 40% protein, 30% carbs, 30% fat, high fiber
- Meal structure: Intermittent fasting 16:8, 3 meals within eating window
- Focus foods: Lean proteins, non-starchy vegetables, healthy fats, whole grains
- Prep strategy: Sunday batch cooking, portioned containers, emergency snacks prepared
