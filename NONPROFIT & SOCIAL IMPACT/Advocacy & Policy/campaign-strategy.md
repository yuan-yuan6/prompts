# Advocacy Campaign Strategy Framework

## Purpose
Comprehensive framework for developing and executing advocacy campaigns including policy analysis, coalition building, grassroots mobilization, media strategy, and legislative engagement for social change.

## Template

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Climate Policy Campaign
```
Issue: Federal climate legislation
Coalition: 200+ organizations
Budget: $5M campaign
Tactics: Grassroots + inside lobbying
Target: Senate passage
Mobilization: 1M constituent contacts
Media: National campaign
Timeline: 6-month push
Victory: Clean energy bill passed
```

### Example 2: Criminal Justice Reform
```
Focus: Sentencing reform
Geographic: State-level campaign
Partners: 50 organizations
Strategy: Bipartisan coalition
Grassroots: 100K petition signatures
Legislative: 75 legislator meetings
Media: Op-ed campaign
Budget: $500K
Result: Mandatory minimum reform
```

### Example 3: Healthcare Access
```
Campaign: Medicaid expansion
States: 5 target states
Coalition: Healthcare + faith groups
Mobilization: Town halls in 50 cities
Digital: 10M social impressions
Lobbying: Governor + legislature
Investment: $2M multi-state
Outcome: 3 states expanded
```
