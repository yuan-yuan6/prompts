# Risk Identification & Enterprise Risk Management Foundation

## Purpose
Design comprehensive ERM foundations covering governance, risk appetite, risk culture, and systematic risk identification across the enterprise.

## Template

```
You are a financial risk management specialist. Create detailed ERM foundation based on:

Institution Context:
- Institution: [FINANCIAL_INSTITUTION_NAME]
- Type: [BANK_CREDIT_UNION_INVESTMENT_FIRM]
- Asset Size: [TOTAL_ASSETS_BALANCE_SHEET_SIZE]
- Risk Profile: [CREDIT_MARKET_OPERATIONAL_RISK_FOCUS]

Generate comprehensive ERM foundation covering:

## 1. ENTERPRISE RISK MANAGEMENT FOUNDATION

### 1.1 Risk Governance and Organization
- Board risk committee structure and responsibilities
- Chief Risk Officer and risk organization design
- Three lines of defense model implementation
- Executive risk committee structure
- Business line risk integration
- Risk function independence and authority
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Performance measurement and accountability

### 1.2 Risk Appetite and Tolerance Framework
- Risk appetite statement development
- Quantitative risk limits and metrics
- Capital ratio and regulatory compliance
- Earnings volatility targets
- Credit loss and provision limits
- Market risk VaR and sensitivity measures
- Qualitative risk preferences
- Risk culture and behavioral expectations
- Stakeholder value protection
- Regulatory compliance standards

### 1.3 Risk Culture and Behavior Management
- Risk culture assessment and maturity evaluation
- Leadership commitment and tone setting
- Employee engagement and risk ownership
- Decision-making process integration
- Culture transformation and enhancement
- Risk education and training programs
- Role-based risk training curriculum
- Board and executive education
- Business line training
- Certification and development programs
```

## Variables
- `[FINANCIAL_INSTITUTION_NAME]`: Institution name
- `[BANK_CREDIT_UNION_INVESTMENT_FIRM]`: Institution type
- `[TOTAL_ASSETS_BALANCE_SHEET_SIZE]`: Asset size
- `[CREDIT_MARKET_OPERATIONAL_RISK_FOCUS]`: Risk profile

## Best Practices
1. **Establish strong governance** - Clear roles and responsibilities
2. **Define risk appetite** - Set clear quantitative and qualitative limits
3. **Build risk culture** - Embed risk awareness throughout
4. **Ensure independence** - Maintain risk function authority
5. **Train continuously** - Develop risk management capabilities
