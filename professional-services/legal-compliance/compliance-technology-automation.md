## Best Practices
1. **Start with high-value use cases** - Automate processes with clear ROI
2. **Integrate systems** - Ensure seamless data flow across platforms
3. **Leverage AI responsibly** - Address bias and explainability
4. **Enable self-service** - Empower users with analytics tools
5. **Monitor continuously** - Track system performance and adoption
