## Variables
- `[ORGANIZATION_NAME]`: Organization name
- `[THIRD_PARTY_PORTFOLIO_SIZE]`: Number of vendors
- `[THIRD_PARTY_RISK_CATEGORIES]`: Vendor risk areas

## Best Practices
1. **Assess risk continuously** - Monitor vendor compliance and performance
2. **Manage contracts effectively** - Track obligations and SLAs
3. **Measure performance** - Use KPIs and regular assessments
4. **Plan for incidents** - Have response protocols ready
5. **Drive improvement** - Work collaboratively with vendors
