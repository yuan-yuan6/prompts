# Investment Portfolio Optimization Template

## Purpose
Optimize investment portfolios through strategic asset allocation, capital budgeting, risk-return optimization, and portfolio rebalancing to maximize value creation and achieve strategic objectives.

## Variables
- `[COMPANY_NAME]`: Organization name
- `[INDUSTRY_SECTOR]`: Industry classification
- `[CAPITAL_BUDGET_LIMITS]`: Total capital available
- `[INVESTMENT_TIMEFRAME]`: Investment horizon
- `[INVESTMENT_RISK_LEVEL]`: Risk profile
- `[WACC_RATE]`: Cost of capital
- `[HURDLE_RATE]`: Required return
- `[CURRENT_INVESTMENTS]`: Existing portfolio
- `[INVESTMENT_PIPELINE]`: Potential investments
- `[STRATEGIC_OBJECTIVES]`: Strategic priorities
- `[RESOURCE_LIMITS]`: Resource constraints
- `[MARKET_ENVIRONMENT]`: Market conditions
- `[ECONOMIC_FORECAST]`: Economic outlook
- `[COMPETITIVE_SITUATION]`: Competitive landscape

## Usage Example
Use for portfolio optimization, capital allocation, investment prioritization, budget planning, or strategic resource allocation.

## Customization Tips
- Adapt asset categories to industry and company context
- Include strategic objectives and non-financial criteria
- Add resource constraints beyond capital (talent, capacity)
- Incorporate ESG and sustainability considerations
- Include dynamic rebalancing based on market conditions
- Add scenario planning for uncertain environments
