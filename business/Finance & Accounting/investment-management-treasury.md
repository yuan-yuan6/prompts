# Investment Management (Treasury)

## Purpose
Manage short-term investment portfolio, optimize returns within risk constraints, and ensure compliance with investment policies.

## Template

```
### 1. INVESTMENT POLICY
Policy Parameters:
- Maximum maturity: [MAXIMUM_INVESTMENT_MATURITY] days
- Minimum credit rating: [MINIMUM_CREDIT_RATING]
- Concentration limits: [CONCENTRATION_LIMITS]
- Eligible instruments: [ELIGIBLE_INVESTMENT_INSTRUMENTS]

### 2. PORTFOLIO ALLOCATION
Asset Allocation:
- Money market funds: [MONEY_MARKET_ALLOCATION]%
- Treasury securities: [TREASURY_ALLOCATION]%
- Commercial paper: [COMMERCIAL_PAPER_ALLOCATION]%
- Certificates of deposit: [CD_ALLOCATION]%
- Corporate bonds: [CORPORATE_BOND_ALLOCATION]%

### 3. PERFORMANCE METRICS
Target Returns:
- Benchmark index: [BENCHMARK_INDEX]
- Target return: [TARGET_RETURN_RATE]%
- Risk-adjusted return: [RISK_ADJUSTED_RETURN_TARGET]
- Tracking error: [TRACKING_ERROR_TOLERANCE] bps

### 4. COUNTERPARTY MANAGEMENT
Approved Counterparties:
- Credit limits by counterparty: [COUNTERPARTY_LIMITS]
- Concentration limits: [COUNTERPARTY_CONCENTRATION]
- Review frequency: [COUNTERPARTY_REVIEW_FREQUENCY]
```

## Best Practices
- Invest only in approved instruments
- Diversify across counterparties
- Match investment maturities to cash needs
- Monitor market conditions daily
- Rebalance portfolio regularly
- Document investment decisions
