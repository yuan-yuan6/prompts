## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Elementary Classroom
```
Grade: 3rd Grade
Students: 24 (3 IEP, 4 ELL)
Subjects: All core subjects
Focus: Reading improvement, behavior management
Approach: Centers-based learning
Technology: 1:1 Chromebooks
Parent Engagement: Weekly newsletters, monthly meetings
```

### Example 2: Middle School Math
```
Grade: 7th Grade Math
Students: 28 per class, 5 classes
Levels: Regular, Advanced, Intervention
Focus: Problem-solving, growth mindset
Assessment: Daily exit tickets, weekly quizzes
Technology: Interactive whiteboard, graphing calculators
Differentiation: Tiered assignments
```

### Example 3: High School Special Education
```
Setting: Resource Room
Students: 12 (all IEP)
Subjects: Multi-subject support
Focus: Executive functioning, self-advocacy
Supports: Modified assignments, assistive technology
Collaboration: Co-teaching, regular ed consultation
Transition: Post-secondary preparation
```
