# Liquidity Planning

## Purpose
Develop comprehensive liquidity strategy, conduct stress testing, establish contingency funding plans, and optimize liquidity buffer levels.

## Template

```
### 1. LIQUIDITY STRATEGY
Target Liquidity Metrics:
- Minimum liquidity buffer: $[MINIMUM_LIQUIDITY_BUFFER]
- Target liquidity ratio: [TARGET_LIQUIDITY_RATIO]%
- Quick ratio target: [QUICK_RATIO_TARGET]
- Current ratio target: [CURRENT_RATIO_TARGET]

### 2. FUNDING SOURCES
Available Funding:
- Cash and equivalents: $[CASH_EQUIVALENTS]
- Credit facilities: $[CREDIT_FACILITY_TOTAL]
- Commercial paper: $[COMMERCIAL_PAPER_CAPACITY]
- Undrawn facilities: $[UNDRAWN_FACILITIES]

### 3. STRESS TESTING
Scenario Analysis:
- Baseline scenario: [BASELINE_SCENARIO_DESCRIPTION]
- Mild stress scenario: [MILD_STRESS_SCENARIO]
- Severe stress scenario: [SEVERE_STRESS_SCENARIO]
- Combined stress scenario: [COMBINED_STRESS_SCENARIO]

### 4. CONTINGENCY PLANNING
Contingency Measures:
- Asset liquidation plan: [ASSET_LIQUIDATION_PLAN]
- Emergency funding sources: [EMERGENCY_FUNDING_SOURCES]
- Cost reduction triggers: [COST_REDUCTION_TRIGGERS]
- Communication protocols: [CRISIS_COMMUNICATION_PLAN]
```

## Best Practices
- Maintain adequate liquidity buffers
- Conduct regular stress testing
- Diversify funding sources
- Document contingency plans
- Monitor early warning indicators
- Test contingency procedures regularly
