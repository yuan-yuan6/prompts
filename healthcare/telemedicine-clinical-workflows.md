# Telemedicine Clinical Workflows & Provider Operations

## Purpose
Design comprehensive telemedicine clinical workflows covering provider operations, scheduling, documentation, and care coordination.

## Template

```
You are a telemedicine operations specialist. Create detailed clinical workflows based on:

Clinical Context:
- Care Model: [TELEMEDICINE_DELIVERY_MODEL]
- Provider Types: [CLINICAL_STAFF_ROLES]
- Visit Types: [CONSULTATION_TYPES_OFFERED]

Generate comprehensive workflow guidance covering:

## 3. CLINICAL WORKFLOWS AND OPERATIONS

### 3.1 Provider Operations Model
- Provider scheduling and availability
- Coverage model and staffing ratios
- Credentialing and privileging
- Training and competency assessment
- Performance evaluation and feedback
- Compensation and productivity models
- Burnout prevention and support
- Professional development opportunities

### 3.2 Visit Workflow Design
- Pre-visit preparation and intake
- Patient identity verification
- Clinical documentation requirements
- Physical examination techniques
- Diagnostic assessment and testing
- Treatment planning and prescribing
- Care coordination and referrals
- Follow-up scheduling and monitoring

### 3.3 Scheduling and Access Management
- Appointment scheduling systems
- Same-day and urgent access
- Provider panel and capacity management
- Wait time monitoring and optimization
- Patient reminder and confirmation
- No-show reduction strategies
- Cancellation and rescheduling policies
- After-hours coverage models

### 3.4 Clinical Documentation
- Visit note templates and structure
- Time-based vs. comprehensive documentation
- Audio/video recording policies
- E-signature and authentication
- Documentation completion timeliness
- Quality review and audit processes
- Billing and coding accuracy
- Legal and regulatory compliance

### 3.5 Care Coordination
- Referral management and tracking
- Specialist consultation coordination
- Lab and imaging order management
- Medication management and pharmacy
- Care team communication protocols
- Transition of care processes
- Community resource connections
- Patient navigation support
```

## Variables
- `[TELEMEDICINE_DELIVERY_MODEL]`: Care delivery model
- `[CLINICAL_STAFF_ROLES]`: Provider types
- `[CONSULTATION_TYPES_OFFERED]`: Visit types

## Best Practices
1. **Design efficient workflows** - Minimize provider burden
2. **Standardize processes** - Use templates and protocols
3. **Coordinate care effectively** - Ensure seamless transitions
4. **Document appropriately** - Balance quality and efficiency
5. **Optimize scheduling** - Maximize access and capacity
