# Content Discovery & Source Monitoring Template

## Purpose
Create systematic content discovery and source monitoring strategies for identifying, tracking, and evaluating potential content for digital libraries and knowledge repositories.

## Variables
- `[SUBJECT_DOMAIN_FOCUS]`: Primary subject areas
- `[CONTENT_TYPE_SPECIFICATIONS]`: Types of content needed
- `[COLLECTION_SCOPE_DEFINITION]`: Scope parameters
- `[QUALITY_CRITERIA_FRAMEWORK]`: Quality standards
- `[MONITORING_FREQUENCY]`: How often to check sources

## Best Practices
1. **Diversify sources** - Don't rely on single channels
2. **Automate where possible** - Scale discovery with technology
3. **Engage experts** - Leverage subject matter knowledge
4. **Track performance** - Measure source effectiveness
5. **Stay current** - Monitor emerging platforms
6. **Balance breadth and depth** - Cover widely but maintain quality
7. **Document processes** - Ensure repeatability
8. **Iterate and improve** - Refine based on results
