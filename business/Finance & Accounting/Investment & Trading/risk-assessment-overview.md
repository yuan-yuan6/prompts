# Risk Assessment Overview

## Purpose
Overview of comprehensive risk assessment framework covering identification, analysis, measurement, mitigation, and ongoing management.

## Framework Components

### 1. Risk Identification & Analysis ([risk-identification-analysis.md](risk-identification-analysis.md))
- Market, credit, operational, compliance, strategic risks
- Qualitative and quantitative analysis
- Risk register development

### 2. Risk Measurement & Quantification ([risk-measurement-quantification.md](risk-measurement-quantification.md))
- VaR and risk metrics
- Stress testing and scenarios
- Statistical models and simulation

### 3. Risk Mitigation & Management ([risk-mitigation-management.md](risk-mitigation-management.md))
- Mitigation strategies (avoid, reduce, transfer, accept)
- Control frameworks
- Monitoring and reporting

## Process Flow
1. Identify risks comprehensively
2. Analyze and prioritize risks
3. Quantify risk exposure
4. Develop mitigation strategies
5. Implement controls
6. Monitor and report continuously
7. Review and improve

## Best Practices
- Use multiple identification methods
- Quantify risks where possible
- Balance cost and benefit of mitigation
- Monitor key risk indicators
- Report regularly to stakeholders
- Review and update continuously
