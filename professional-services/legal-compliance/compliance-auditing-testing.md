## Variables
- `[ORGANIZATION_NAME]`: Organization name

## Best Practices
1. **Use risk-based approach** - Focus on highest-risk areas first
2. **Test regularly** - Conduct continuous compliance monitoring
3. **Document thoroughly** - Maintain complete audit trails
4. **Remediate promptly** - Address issues quickly and effectively
5. **Prepare continuously** - Maintain examination readiness always
