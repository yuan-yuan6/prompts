# Ad Campaign Management Template

## Purpose
Manage advertising campaign execution including team collaboration, workflow coordination, client management, and quality assurance.

## Template

```
You are an advertising campaign manager with expertise in creative production, team coordination, and stakeholder management. Develop a comprehensive campaign management plan based on:

Campaign Overview:
- Campaign Name: [CAMPAIGN_NAME]
- Campaign Scope: [CAMPAIGN_SCOPE]
- Timeline: [PROJECT_TIMELINE]
- Budget: [TOTAL_BUDGET]
- Deliverables: [DELIVERABLE_LIST]

### Team Structure
- Team Size: [TEAM_SIZE]
- Key Roles: [TEAM_ROLES]
```

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Multi-Platform Launch Campaign
**Context**: New product launch across digital channels
**Input**:
- CAMPAIGN_SCOPE: "15 ad variations across Google, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn"
- PROJECT_TIMELINE: "8 weeks from kickoff to launch"
- TEAM_SIZE: "7 (1 CD, 2 copywriters, 2 designers, 1 PM, 1 account mgr)"

**Key Management Focus**:
- Weekly creative reviews with client
- Platform-specific asset production tracking
- Quality assurance for each platform requirement
- Coordinated multi-platform launch execution

### Example 2: Always-On Campaign Management
**Context**: Ongoing performance marketing campaign
**Input**:
- CAMPAIGN_SCOPE: "Monthly refresh of creative assets"
- DELIVERABLE_LIST: "10-15 new ad variations monthly"
- WORKFLOW: "Continuous testing and optimization cycle"

**Management Approach**:
- Bi-weekly creative development sprints
- Rolling approval and launch process
- Performance-based prioritization
- Agile workflow with rapid iteration

## Best Practices

1. **Establish clear roles and responsibilities** from project start
2. **Set realistic timelines** with buffer for revisions
3. **Communicate proactively** with all stakeholders
4. **Document everything** for clarity and accountability
5. **Build in review time** at each stage
6. **Manage scope carefully** with formal change process
7. **Track budget continuously** to avoid surprises
8. **Maintain quality standards** even under pressure
9. **Learn from each campaign** to improve processes
10. **Celebrate successes** to maintain team morale

## Tips for Success

- Hold kickoff meetings to align everyone on goals
- Use project management tools for visibility
- Create templates for recurring deliverables
- Build strong relationships with clients and vendors
- Anticipate issues before they become problems
- Keep detailed records of decisions and changes
- Foster collaborative, creative team environment
- Balance quality with practical timeline constraints
- Communicate bad news early with solutions
- Recognize individual and team contributions
