## Variables
- `[ARTIST_PROFILE]`: Artist or professional background
- `[GENRE_MARKET]`: Genre and target market

## Best Practices
1. **Start with strategy** - Plan distribution before release
2. **Optimize metadata** - Ensure discoverability on all platforms
3. **Track everything** - Use analytics to guide decisions
4. **Build relationships** - Network is crucial in music industry
5. **Diversify revenue** - Don't rely on single income source
6. **Engage your audience** - Build genuine fan relationships
7. **Stay professional** - Treat music as a business
8. **Think long-term** - Build sustainable career, not quick wins
