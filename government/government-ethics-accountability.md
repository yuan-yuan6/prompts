# Government Ethics & Accountability

## Purpose
Framework for implementing government ethics programs, preventing conflicts of interest, managing financial disclosures, ethics training, enforcement, and building culture of integrity.

## Quick Start

### Minimal Example
```
Implement ethics program for state agency including conflict of interest policies, financial disclosure system, gifts and hospitality rules, post-employment restrictions, ethics training, hotline for violations, and enforcement procedures.
```

### Basic 3-Step Workflow
1. **Program Development** - Policies, procedures, systems (2-3 months)
2. **Training & Implementation** - Staff education, disclosure filing (3-6 months)
3. **Monitoring & Enforcement** - Compliance, investigations, sanctions (ongoing)

---

## Key Components

- Conflict of interest policies
- Financial disclosure requirements
- Gifts and gratuities rules
- Post-employment restrictions
- Ethics training programs
- Reporting mechanisms and hotlines
- Investigation procedures
- Enforcement and sanctions
