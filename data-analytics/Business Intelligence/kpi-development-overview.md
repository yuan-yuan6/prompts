# KPI Development & Management Overview

## Purpose
Overview of comprehensive KPI development and management covering strategic framework, metric design, implementation, and continuous monitoring.

## Framework Components

### 1. Strategic Framework ([kpi-strategic-framework.md](kpi-strategic-framework.md))
**Focus:** Strategy alignment and architecture
- Strategic goal alignment
- KPI hierarchy and levels
- Balanced scorecard design
- Stakeholder alignment
**Use when:** Developing KPI strategy or aligning metrics to goals.

### 2. Design & Metrics ([kpi-design-metrics.md](kpi-design-metrics.md))
**Focus:** KPI identification and design
- KPI discovery and selection
- Metric definition and formulas
- Target setting
- Validation
**Use when:** Designing new KPIs or defining metrics.

### 3. Implementation & Monitoring ([kpi-implementation-monitoring.md](kpi-implementation-monitoring.md))
**Focus:** Deployment and tracking
- Data infrastructure
- Dashboard development
- Performance monitoring
- Continuous improvement
**Use when:** Implementing KPIs or building dashboards.

## Best Practices
1. Align KPIs to strategy
2. Keep number focused (5-7 per level)
3. Balance leading and lagging indicators
4. Set SMART targets
5. Automate data collection
6. Review and refine regularly
