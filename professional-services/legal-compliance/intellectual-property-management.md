## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Technology Company IP Strategy
```
Company: Software/AI technology company
IP Portfolio: 150 patents, 25 trademarks
Budget: $5M annually
Focus: Patent prosecution, trade secret protection
Goals: Defensive patent portfolio, licensing revenue
Revenue Target: $10M from IP licensing
```

### Example 2: Life Sciences IP Management
```
Company: Pharmaceutical/biotech company
IP Portfolio: 75 patents, 15 trademarks
Budget: $12M annually
Focus: Drug patents, regulatory exclusivity
Goals: Patent term extensions, orphan drug status
Revenue Target: $500M protected revenue
```

### Example 3: Consumer Brand Protection
```
Company: Consumer goods manufacturer
IP Portfolio: 200 trademarks, 50 design patents
Budget: $8M annually
Focus: Brand protection, anti-counterfeiting
Goals: Global trademark portfolio, enforcement
Revenue Target: $2B brand value protection
```
