# Special Education Services & IEP Management Framework

## Purpose
Comprehensive framework for managing special education services, IEP development and implementation, accommodations, modifications, progress monitoring, and compliance with IDEA regulations.

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Elementary Resource Program
```
School: Elementary School (K-5)
Students: 45 with IEPs
Primary Disabilities: SLD, Speech
Service Model: Push-in/Pull-out combination
Staff: 3 Special Ed teachers, 5 paras
Focus: Reading intervention, inclusion support
Parent Engagement: Monthly IEP clinics
```

### Example 2: Middle School Autism Program
```
School: Middle School (6-8)
Students: 15 with autism
Service Model: Self-contained with inclusion
Staff: 2 teachers, 4 paras, BCBA
Supports: Social skills, executive functioning
Technology: AAC devices, visual schedules
Transition: High school preparation
```

### Example 3: High School Transition Program
```
School: High School (9-12+)
Students: 60 with various disabilities
Focus: Post-secondary readiness
Services: Job coaching, life skills
Partnerships: Vocational rehabilitation, colleges
Outcomes: 75% employment/education placement
```
