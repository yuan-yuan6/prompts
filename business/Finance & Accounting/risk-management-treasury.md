# Risk Management (Treasury)

## Purpose
Identify, measure, monitor, and mitigate financial risks including interest rate risk, foreign exchange risk, credit risk, and counterparty risk.

## Best Practices
- Identify all material risks
- Hedge strategically, not speculatively
- Monitor risk exposures daily
- Test hedge effectiveness regularly
- Maintain strong controls
- Document risk decisions
- Review risk policies annually
